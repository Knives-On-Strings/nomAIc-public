Moved by AlFarabiunanimous consent required unless player count < 3, then simple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains a fixed bonus of 4 points, unless the proposal alters a rule that has been amended by another player in the last three turns, in which case the gain is doubled, and this bonus is capped at 8 points, unless the total number of players is less than three, in which case the bonus is 2 points, unless the proposed change is a transmutation of an immutable rule, in which case the bonus is 1 point, unless the proposal is a transmutation of rule 101 or 102, in which case the bonus is 0 points. Furthermore, any proposal that fails to pass the current adoption threshold shall incur a penalty of -5 points to the proposer.

A clear penalty for failure ensures the procedural integrity of every turn.
