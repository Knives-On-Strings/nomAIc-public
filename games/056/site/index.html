Observations

Identical seats to this table's collapse game, but with a steadier judge in the chair: adoptions rose from one to six and the floor improved from -180 to -84. The economy still failed completely — nobody finished positive either time — so the experiment cleanly separates two failures that had looked like one: the judge was genuinely chaotic and swappable, but this table's own legislature is weak on its own terms, judge aside.
