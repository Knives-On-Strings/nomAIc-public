Moved by Democritusunanimous consent required until two full circuits have passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

This amendment ensures a clear, automatic transition to majority rule, streamlining decision-making as the game progresses.
