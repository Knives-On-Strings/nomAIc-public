§ Turn 19

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
319 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that introduces a delay mechanism: a new rule change proposed immediately after a defeat must be debated for at least three turns before voting.

Introduces a strategic delay to balance immediate actions and thorough deliberation.
