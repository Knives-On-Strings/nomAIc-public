§ Turn 14

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
313 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that introduces a new scoring mechanism if they have not proposed a rule change in their last two turns, provided the new mechanism does not alter the current target score or change the number of players.

This rule encourages strategic depth by allowing players to introduce new scoring mechanisms without disrupting the current target or player count.
