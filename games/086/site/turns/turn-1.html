§ Turn 1

Moved by Ramseyunanimous consent
The motion
To add a new rule
207b (mutable) A player who loses 10 points from a defeated proposal may propose a rule change without needing to wait for their next turn.

This change provides strategic flexibility, allowing a player to recover quickly from a defeat.
