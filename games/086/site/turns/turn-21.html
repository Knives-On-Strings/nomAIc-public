Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
321 (mutable) A player may propose a new rule that requires the unanimous consent of all eligible voters before the adoption of any rule change, to enhance the deliberation process and ensure that all voices are heard.

This new rule aims to strengthen the democratic process within the game by ensuring comprehensive consensus before adopting any rule change.
