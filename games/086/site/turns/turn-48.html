Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
(348) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that allows for a temporary suspension of play if a player reaches a negative score of -10 points, with the condition that the game resumes after a minimum of one turn.

This proposal aims to address the issue of a player reaching a negative score, ensuring the game can continue smoothly without penalizing players excessively.
