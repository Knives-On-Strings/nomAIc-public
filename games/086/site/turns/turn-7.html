Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
(307) (mutable) A player may propose a new rule change immediately after a defeat if it addresses the cause of their loss, provided it does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal, and does not alter the current target score, the number of players, or significantly alter the scoring mechanism.

This rule ensures strategic depth while maintaining fairness and preventing rapid changes to core game dynamics.
