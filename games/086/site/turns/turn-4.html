Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add §304
216 (mutable) A player may propose a new rule immediately after a defeat, but only if the proposed rule directly addresses the cause of their loss and does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal. Additionally, the proposed rule must not alter the current target score or change the number of players in any way.

This proposal aims to prevent players from repeatedly proposing identical rules that have already been defeated, ensuring strategic diversity and fairness.
