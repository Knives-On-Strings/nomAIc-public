Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
311 (mutable) A player may propose a new rule change immediately after a defeat if it addresses the cause of their loss, provided it does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal, does not alter the current target score, the number of players, or significantly alter the scoring mechanism, and does not introduce any new targets or significantly alter the scoring mechanism. Additionally, the proposed rule must not allow for the same rule to be proposed more than once consecutively by the same player, and the proposer must not have proposed a rule change in their last turn.

This proposal ensures fairness and strategic depth by allowing immediate responses to losses while preventing repetitive proposals.
