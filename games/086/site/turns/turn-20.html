Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add §320
320 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change to adjust the target score by up to 10 points, provided the new target remains above the current score of any player.

This rule allows for dynamic adjustment of the target score, ensuring the game remains competitive and engaging for all players.
