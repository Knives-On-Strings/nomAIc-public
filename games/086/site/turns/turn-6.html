§ Turn 6

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add §306
218 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it directly addresses the cause of their loss, provided it does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal, and does not alter the current target score or change the number of players. Additionally, the proposed rule must not introduce any new targets or significantly alter the scoring mechanism.

This addition ensures strategic depth while maintaining fairness and balance, preventing excessive disruption.
