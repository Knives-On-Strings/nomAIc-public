§ Turn 17

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add §317
317 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it ensures that the game remains balanced and strategically deep, but only if it has been reviewed by another player.

To maintain strategic depth and balance while ensuring checks through peer review.

RamseyAs Ramsey, I propose adding the following rule: "317 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it ensures that the game remains balanced and strategically deep, but only if it has been reviewed by another player." This proposal aims to maintain the strategic depth of the game while ensuring balance. It requires that any proposed rule change be reviewed by another player, thus promoting careful consideration and collaboration. This addition will prevent hasty or unbalanced changes that could disrupt the integrity of the game. Pass.
