The motion
To add §339
(339) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that modifies the target score only if it is unanimously agreed upon by all eligible voters, ensuring that score adjustments are consensual.

Ensures that any adjustments to the target score require full consensus, maintaining fairness and balance.
