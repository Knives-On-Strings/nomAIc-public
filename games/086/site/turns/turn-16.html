§ Turn 16

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
315 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change to increase the target score by up to 10 points, provided they have not proposed a rule change in their last turn.

This change allows players to adapt the target score to prolong the game and add strategic depth.
