The motion
To add a new rule
343 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that reduces the target score by up to 20 points, provided the target remains above 100, in order to strategically extend the game duration and promote more intricate play.

To strategically prolong gameplay and enhance strategic depth by allowing players to adjust the target score, thus preventing premature conclusions and encouraging prolonged tactical planning.
