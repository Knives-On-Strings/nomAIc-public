Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
312 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it addresses the cause of their loss, provided it does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal, does not alter the current target score, the number of players, or significantly alter the scoring mechanism, and does not introduce any new targets or significantly alter the scoring mechanism. Additionally, the proposed rule must not allow for the same rule to be proposed more than once consecutively by the same player, the proposer must not have proposed a rule change in their last turn, and the proposer must not propose a rule that has been defeated in the last three turns.

This rule adds a safeguard against repetitive strategic manipulation while allowing responsive rule changes.
