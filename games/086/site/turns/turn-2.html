§ Turn 2

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
214 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it directly addresses the cause of their loss, provided it does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal.

This rule aims to enhance strategic depth by allowing players to swiftly address and mitigate losses from their immediate past actions without repeating previous failed attempts.
