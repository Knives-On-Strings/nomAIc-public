Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add §310
310 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat, provided it addresses the cause of their loss and does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal, does not alter the current target score, the number of players, or significantly alter the scoring mechanism, and does not introduce any new targets or significantly alter the scoring mechanism. Additionally, the proposed rule must not allow for the same rule to be proposed more than once consecutively by the same player.

To prevent repetitive rule proposals by the same player immediately after defeat, ensuring a more dynamic and varied strategic environment.
