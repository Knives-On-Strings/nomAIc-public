Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
(347) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change to adjust the target score by up to 5 points, subject to approval by unanimous consent.

This change allows for strategic flexibility in adjusting the target score, provided there is unanimous agreement among all players.
