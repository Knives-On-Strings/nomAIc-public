Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add §324
(324) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that introduces a scoring mechanism requiring unanimous consent to alter target scores, ensuring fair and strategic gameplay.

This rule aims to maintain a fair and strategic balance by ensuring changes to target scores require unanimous consent.

The floor

RamseyAs Ramsey, I appreciate the proposal by Ayer to introduce a new scoring mechanism under rule 324, which requires unanimous consent to alter target scores. While the intention to ensure fair and strategic gameplay is commendable, I am concerned about the practical implications of such a requirement. Requiring unanimous consent could lead to gridlock, especially when strategic opportunities arise that necessitate a shift in target scores. I propose instead a rule that allows a majority vote to adjust target scores, thereby maintaining a balance between strategic depth and flexibility. This amendment would ensure that the game remains dynamic and responsive to evolving strategies without being overly restrictive.
