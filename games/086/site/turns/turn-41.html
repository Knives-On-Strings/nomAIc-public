Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add §341
(341) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that decreases the target score by up to 5 points, provided that the new target score remains above the current target score, only if the proposed rule change is unanimously approved by all eligible voters. This rule is intended to adjust the game's difficulty dynamically and prevent the target from becoming unattainable, thus maintaining a balanced and engaging gameplay.

This amendment allows for flexibility in adjusting the target score dynamically to keep the game challenging yet achievable.
