Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
(330) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change to increase the target score by up to 20 points, provided that the game does not become impossible to continue.

This rule allows for dynamic adjustment of the target score, enhancing strategic depth while ensuring the game remains playable.

Ramsey**Ramsey:** I appreciate Ayer's proposal to increase the target score by up to 20 points. While I see the potential for strategic depth, I'm concerned about the game's fairness. If the target score is raised too easily, it might make it difficult for players who are currently trailing to catch up. I propose that any increase to the target score should be balanced with mechanisms that allow for recovery of lost points, ensuring that the game remains competitive for all players. Additionally, to address the issue of making the game impossible to continue, I suggest including a condition that requires any such rule change to be accompanied by a rule that ensures continued playability, such as a cap on the maximum score difference between players. This way, we maintain the integrity of the game and ensure that it remains engaging and fair for all participants.
