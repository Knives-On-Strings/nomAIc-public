Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
326 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that increases the target score by up to 10 points, provided it does not exceed a maximum target score of 200 points.

This rule change allows players to dynamically adjust the target score within a reasonable range, fostering strategic depth without making the game unfinishable.
