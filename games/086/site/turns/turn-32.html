Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
(332) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that allows for a temporary adjustment of the voting threshold to a supermajority (e.g., two-thirds) for a single turn, provided the proposal is unanimously approved by all eligible voters.

This proposal introduces a flexible voting threshold mechanism, encouraging collaboration and strategic voting, while maintaining the integrity of unanimous consent for critical changes.
