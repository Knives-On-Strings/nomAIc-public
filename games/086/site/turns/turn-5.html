Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
217 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it directly addresses the cause of their loss, provided it does not replicate the content of their last defeated proposal and does not alter the current target score or change the number of players.

Ensures that players can address their recent defeat strategically, enhancing fairness and depth while maintaining game balance.
