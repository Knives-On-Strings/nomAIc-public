Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
325 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that introduces a delay mechanism: the proposed change will take effect only after the next player's turn, ensuring broader discussion and preventing immediate strategic shifts. The delay mechanism does not apply to rule transmutations.

Introducing a delay mechanism to ensure comprehensive discussion before changes take effect, promoting thoughtful and collaborative rule evolution.
