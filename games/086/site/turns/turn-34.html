§ Turn 34

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
(333) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change that allows for a new scoring mechanism to be introduced. This rule change must be proposed immediately after a defeat and must address a conflict or enhance strategic depth.

Enhancing strategic depth by allowing new scoring mechanisms to be introduced post-defeat.
