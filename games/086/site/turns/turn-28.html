Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add §329
(329) (mutable) A player may propose a rule change to introduce a new scoring mechanism, provided it maintains the balance between strategic depth and fairness. The new scoring mechanism must be agreed upon unanimously by all players.

Introduces a new scoring mechanism that requires unanimous agreement, ensuring fairness and strategic depth.
