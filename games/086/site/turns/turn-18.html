Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To add §318
318 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change to decrease the target score by up to 10 points once per game, provided they have reached at least 50 points, to increase strategic depth and adaptability.

This proposal enhances strategic depth by allowing players to adjust the target score under specific conditions, encouraging thoughtful play.
