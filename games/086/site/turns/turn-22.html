The motion
To add a new rule
322 (mutable) A player may propose a rule change immediately after a defeat if it enhances strategic depth without increasing the number of mutable rules beyond the limit.

This rule supports strategic depth by allowing immediate rule changes after a defeat, while ensuring the limit on mutable rules is respected.
