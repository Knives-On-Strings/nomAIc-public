Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the square root of the number of mutable rules currently in force, rounded down, with a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 4.

Encourages strategic proposal of rule changes by balancing bonus points more evenly across all players.
