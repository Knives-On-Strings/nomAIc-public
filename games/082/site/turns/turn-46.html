§ Turn 46

Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 2.5, with a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 12.

This amendment balances the point distribution to ensure more consistent scoring opportunities for all players.
