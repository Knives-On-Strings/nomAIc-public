§ Turn 28

Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to twice the number of mutable rules currently in force, rounded down, with a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 6.

This amendment balances the incentive structure, making it more accessible while maintaining strategic engagement.
