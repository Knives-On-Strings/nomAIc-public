Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the number of mutable rules currently in force, with a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 12.

Adjusting the bonus points range incentivizes rule changes and reflects the current dynamics of mutable rules.
