§ Turn 33

Moved by Vennsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 3, rounded down, with a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 10.

This amendment encourages strategic rule changes by aligning the bonus points with the number of mutable rules, fostering fair competition.
