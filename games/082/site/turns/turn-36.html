§ Turn 36

Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 3, rounded down, with a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 9.

This amendment encourages more strategic rule changes by increasing potential bonus points.
