Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, and the proposer loses 10 points. Additionally, the proposer gains 2 points to encourage future engagement and maintain player morale.

This amendment balances the penalty for a failed proposal with a small reward to encourage continued participation.
