§ Turn 38

Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 1.5, with a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 10.

This amendment balances point distribution to encourage strategic play while maintaining engagement.

Instead, I propose an alternative amendment to rule 202: "If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 2, with a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 15." I encourage the group to consider this alternative, which still aligns with our shared goal of maintaining a fair and competitive gameplay environment. Thank you.
