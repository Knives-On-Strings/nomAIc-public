Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. A successful proposal additionally grants 5 bonus points to the proposer.

Encouraging well-considered proposals by adding an incentive for successful amendments.
