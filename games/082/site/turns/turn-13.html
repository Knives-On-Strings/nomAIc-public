§ Turn 13

Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the number of mutable rules currently in force, encouraging more dynamic rule changes.

To incentivize more active participation in rule changes and adapt to the evolving strategic landscape.
