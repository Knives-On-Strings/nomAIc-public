The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 2, with a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 18. Additionally, players may propose a change to the voting threshold once every five turns, requiring a simple majority.

This amendment balances strategic depth with adaptability, encouraging dynamic engagement.
