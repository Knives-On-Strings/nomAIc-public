Moved by DeMorganunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when a simple majority of eligible voters vote aye, except that the threshold remains unanimous consent until each player has completed at least three full turns.

This amendment allows for a more gradual transition to majority rule, fostering stability and consensus-building early in the game.
