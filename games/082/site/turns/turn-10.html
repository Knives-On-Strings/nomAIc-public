§ Turn 10

Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. A successful proposal additionally grants bonus points equal to half the number of mutable rules currently in force, rounded down.

Adjusting bonus points to reflect the current game complexity promotes balanced strategic play.
