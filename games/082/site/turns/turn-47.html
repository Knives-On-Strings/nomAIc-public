§ Turn 47

Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the current number of mutable rules multiplied by 3, with a minimum of 7 and a maximum of 15.

Increasing the bonus points incentivizes more frequent rule changes, adding strategic depth to the game.
