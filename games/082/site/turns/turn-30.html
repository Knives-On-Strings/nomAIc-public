Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when a simple majority of eligible voters vote aye, except that the threshold remains unanimous consent until each player has completed at least four full turns.

This amendment extends the requirement for unanimous consent, promoting further consensus-building and strategic depth.
