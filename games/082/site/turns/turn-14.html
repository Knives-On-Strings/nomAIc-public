§ Turn 14

Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to half the number of mutable rules currently in force, rounded up.

Balances dynamic rule changes with strategic depth.
