The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the number of mutable rules currently in force minus two, rounded up to at least one.

This amendment aligns the bonus with the number of mutable rules, encouraging strategic rule changes without excessive point gains.
