Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to four times the number of mutable rules currently in force, rounded down, with a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 15.

This amendment encourages more frequent rule changes by increasing the potential bonus points, fostering a dynamic gameplay environment.
