Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the number of mutable rules currently in force plus 2, rounded down, with a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 7.

This adjustment encourages strategic proposal-making by increasing potential bonus points while maintaining a balanced point cap.
