Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains bonus points equal to the number of mutable rules currently in force, rounded down, with a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 5.

This amendment aims to increase strategic depth and engagement by enhancing the bonus system.
