§ Turn 4

Moved by Russell-2unanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposal is a transmutation, in which case they gain 15 points. Additionally, if a proposal fails, the proposer may re-propose it in their next turn without penalty.

This amendment encourages innovation by removing the penalty for a failed proposal, allowing players to refine their strategies.
