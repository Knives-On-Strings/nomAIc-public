§ Turn 1

Moved by Frege-2unanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposal is a transmutation, in which case they gain 15 points.

Incentivizes transmutation proposals for greater potential gains.

The floor

Frege-2The proposed amendment introduces a significant incentive for proposing transmutations by awarding 15 points instead of the standard 1-6 points from a die roll. However, this change could destabilize the game's balance by encouraging frequent transmutations, potentially leading to rule changes that are too rapid and undermining the strategic depth of the game. To preserve the integrity of the game's structure and maintain a measured pace of rule changes, it would be prudent to limit the frequency of transmutations or adjust the point reward to a less drastic increase. This approach ensures that while players are still incentivized to propose transmutations, the core mechanics and strategic planning remain intact.
