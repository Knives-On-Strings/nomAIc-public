§ Turn 22

Moved by Frege-2simple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Players must complete their turn within three minutes, or forfeit that turn. Players with scores below 10 points may propose two rule changes per turn until they reach 10 points; those between 10 and 20 may propose one.

Promotes fairness and efficiency in gameplay.
