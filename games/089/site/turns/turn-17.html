§ Turn 17

Moved by Russell-2simple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. Additionally, players with scores below 10 points may propose two rule changes per turn until they reach 10 points.

This amendment accelerates my progress by allowing multiple proposals per turn when my score is low.
