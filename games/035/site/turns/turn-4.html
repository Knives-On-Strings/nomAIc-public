The motion
To add a new rule
[216] If a player awards points to another player via rule [214], the proposer of the rule change that granted the points must also receive a number of points equal to the number awarded, determined by a roll of a six-sided die.

This ensures that the exercise of rule [214] remains a collaborative act of distribution rather than a mechanism for a single player to altruistically dump points to avoid the consequences of their own move.
