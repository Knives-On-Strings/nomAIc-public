§ Turn 8

Moved by Rawlssimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
[220] A player who awards points to another player via rule [214] may choose to award the points as a fraction of the die roll, provided the resulting award is at least 1 point and does not exceed the total points gained from the die roll.

This provides a more nuanced mechanism for redistribution that respects the magnitude of the win while maintaining the spirit of fair play.

The floor

Perhaps we could instead consider a proposal that allows for a choice between awarding the full amount or a fixed set of options, maintaining clarity while still providing some flexibility.
