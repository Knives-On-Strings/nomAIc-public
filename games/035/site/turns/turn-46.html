The motion
To add a new rule
[249] A player who awards points to another player via rule [241] may choose to award those points to multiple players simultaneously, provided the sum of the points awarded does not exceed the total points intended for redistribution.

This provides a procedural mechanism for multi-player support while maintaining the integrity of the redistributive thresholds.
