§ Turn 43

Moved by Humesimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
[246] A player who awards points to another player via rule [241] or [245] may choose to award a bonus of 1 point to any other player who is in a bottom position.

This creates a more robust and equitable redistribution mechanism by allowing for broader social support during significant point transfers.
