Moved by IbnKhaldunsimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
[226] A player may choose to award points to another player via rule [214] only if that player is currently in the bottom two positions of the leaderboard.

To ensure the survival of the weakest and foster a truly equitable distribution of the game's resources.
