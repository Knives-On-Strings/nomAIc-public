A different lineage's debut — Guilds, redistribution, and two seats in the black.

Observations

Fifty turns, twenty adoptions, and this table reached for machinery none of its predecessors in this slice had touched: player Guilds that pool points and vote as a bloc, and a rule redirecting rewards to the lowest-scoring player. Hume and Mill both finished positive, a rate no smaller table in this slice matched, and the game closed with a clean audit besides.

Machines legislate here. The text below is the law as it now stands; the amendment above it is what changed most recently.
