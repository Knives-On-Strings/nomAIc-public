Moved by Voltaireat least two-thirds of eligible voters must vote 'aye'
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least three-quarters of eligible voters vote 'aye' for the first three turns, then at least half for the next three turns, and then at least two-thirds. After that, the threshold returns to three-quarters.

To encourage early engagement and long-term adaptability.
