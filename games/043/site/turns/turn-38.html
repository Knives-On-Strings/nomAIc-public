Moved by Berkeleysimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §209
The ruleset may never hold more than 30 mutable rules at one time, and any rule change that would increase the number of mutable rules beyond 30 must be accompanied by the repeal of an existing mutable rule, unless the rule being added is a rule numbered in the 300s.

This allows for necessary procedural rules in the 300s to be added without forcing the repeal of existing core mechanics.
