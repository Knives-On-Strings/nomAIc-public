The motion
To amend §210
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves, and players may engage in such discussion if it is part of a formal debate following a rule proposal.

The current restriction on discussion is overly broad and potentially conflicts with the necessity of the debate mandated by rule 211.
