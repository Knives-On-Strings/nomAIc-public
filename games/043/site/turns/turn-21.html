§ Turn 21

Moved by Leibnizsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. If a player's score reaches a value that is a multiple of 10, they may choose to immediately gain or lose 1 point, provided this choice is made before the next player's turn begins.

Introducing a minor, controlled mechanism for score adjustment can facilitate the movement of players through the lower scores and provide a measure of agency within the existing structure.
