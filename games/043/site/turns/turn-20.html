The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that is specifically intended to create a new mechanism for scoring, such as a secondary currency or a way to earn points through non-legislative actions, provided that such a rule does not amend rule 208.

We have refined our legislative procedures; now we must expand our economic possibilities to prevent stagnation.
