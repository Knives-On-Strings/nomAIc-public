The motion
To amend §209
The ruleset may never hold more than 30 mutable rules at one time, provided that any enactment of a new mutable rule is accompanied by the repeal of an existing mutable rule.

I propose synthesizing Pascal's 'one-in, one-out' principle with our current cap of 30 to ensure legislative stability.
