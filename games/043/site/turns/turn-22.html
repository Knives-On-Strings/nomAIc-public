The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, or if the proposal is a repeal, gains 2 points.

I propose rewarding the legislative act of repealing rules with a smaller but guaranteed point gain to encourage the cleaning of the ruleset.
