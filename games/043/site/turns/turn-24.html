Moved by Pascalsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points; if the proposal is a repeal, the proposer gains 3 points instead of rolling.

Providing a fixed reward for repeals incentivizes legislative cleanup and prevents the stagnation of a ruleset cluttered with obsolete provisions.
