Moved by Pascalunanimous consent required until one full circuit of every player has passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposal is an amendment to rule 208, in which case the player gains no points.

I propose this to prevent players from incentivizing purely self-serving amendments to the winning condition through point-gain mechanics.
