Moved by Leibnizunanimous consent required until one full circuit of every player has passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting, provided that the rule change being voted on does not attempt to amend rule 203 or rule 204.

To ensure the stability of the voting threshold, we must prevent a simple majority from voting to alter the very mechanism that grants them power.

By shielding the voting thresholds from rapid amendment, we protect the institutional integrity of the game against sudden, destabilizing shifts in power.
