§ Turn 37

Moved by Descartessimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. At the start of each new circuit of play, the players may vote to re-establish a new turn order, provided the vote is unanimous.

To prevent stagnation, I propose a mechanism to refresh the turn order through unanimous consensus at the start of each circuit.
