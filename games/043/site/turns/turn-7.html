§ Turn 7

Moved by Descartesunanimous consent required until one full circuit of every player has passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take a fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. The turn order is: Leibniz, Descartes, Berkeley, Pascal, Malebranche.

Formalizing the current turn order into the ruleset provides clarity and prevents future disputes regarding the sequence of play.
