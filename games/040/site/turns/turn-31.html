Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. Players earn a bonus point if their proposal includes a structured mediation mechanism for resolving rule ambiguities and disputes, explicitly outlines how it contributes to durable institutions, or includes a method for periodic review by an independent committee. Proposals that explicitly address and resolve previous conflicts or disputes in rule application, or include a mechanism for periodic reassessment of its effects after every 5 turns, receive a bonus point. Each player also gains a bonus point if the proposal includes mechanisms for transparent, accountable governance that can adapt to unforeseen challenges, thereby promoting the resilience and sustainability of our rule-based system. Additionally, proposals that demonstrate a clear understanding of the current ruleset and propose changes that are in line with the principles of durability, transparency, and adaptability will receive a bonus point.

Encourages strategic, adaptable proposals through periodic reassessment.
