The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. Players earn a bonus point if their proposal amends an existing rule to improve clarity or efficiency, includes a review clause for future assessment of the change's impact, provides a clear rationale for the change, enhances long-term stability by incorporating feedback mechanisms, or explicitly outlines how it contributes to durable institutions. Proposals that include a mechanism for periodic assessment and potential adjustment of existing rules, or a clause for automatic reassessment of its effects after a certain number of turns, receive a bonus point. Proposals that explicitly address and resolve previous conflicts or disputes in rule application, or include a mechanism for addressing rule ambiguities and disputes through structured mediation, earn an additional bonus point. Proposals that include a clear rationale for the change receive an extra bonus point. Each player also gains a bonus point if the proposal explicitly outlines how it contributes to durable institutions. Additionally, players earn a bonus point for proposals that include mechanisms for transparent, accountable governance that can adapt to unforeseen challenges, thereby promoting the resilience and sustainability of our rule-based system.

Enhancing resilience and adaptability in our ruleset promotes a sustainable and robust game environment.
