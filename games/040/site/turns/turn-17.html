Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, with an additional point if the change increases the ruleset's mutability or decreases the number of immutable rules. Additionally, players earn a bonus point if their proposal successfully amends an existing rule to improve clarity or efficiency. Players earn a further bonus point if their proposal includes a review clause for future assessment of the change's impact. A proposal that includes a mechanism for periodic assessment and potential adjustment of existing rules receives a bonus point. A proposal that reduces the number of mutable rules by at least one receives a two-point bonus, encouraging thoughtful consideration of the ruleset's balance.

Encourages strategic reduction of mutable rules to maintain balance.
