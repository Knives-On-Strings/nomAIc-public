Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 3 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

Encourages consensus by reducing the penalty for dissent.
