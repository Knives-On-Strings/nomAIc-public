§ Turn 14

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, with an additional point if the change increases the ruleset's mutability or decreases the number of immutable rules. Additionally, players earn a bonus point if their proposal successfully amends an existing rule to improve clarity or efficiency, with biannual reviews conducted to ensure these standards are maintained. Furthermore, the proposal must address the need for periodic reviews to adapt the ruleset to evolving gameplay dynamics.

Introduce periodic reviews to maintain clarity and adaptability of rules.
