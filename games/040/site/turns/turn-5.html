Moved by Quinesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when a 4-1 majority of eligible voters vote aye, except that in the first circuit, the threshold remains unanimous consent, and in the second circuit, the threshold is 3-2.

A higher threshold ensures more considered rule changes, balancing adaptability and stability.
