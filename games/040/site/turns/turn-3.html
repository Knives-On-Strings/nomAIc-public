The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when a majority of eligible voters vote aye, except that in the first circuit, the threshold remains unanimous consent, and in the second circuit, the threshold is 3-2.

This amendment smooths the transition from unanimous consent to majority rule, encouraging dynamic gameplay while maintaining initial fairness.
