Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, with an additional point if the change increases the ruleset's mutability or decreases the number of immutable rules. Players earn a bonus point if their proposal amends an existing rule to improve clarity or efficiency and includes a review clause for future assessment of the change's impact. Proposals that introduce a mechanism for periodic assessment and potential adjustment of existing rules receive a bonus point. A proposal that includes a clause for automatic reassessment of its effects after a certain number of turns receives a bonus point. Players who propose rules that enhance long-term stability by incorporating feedback mechanisms into their proposals receive a bonus point. Additionally, players who propose rules that include a clear rationale for the change receive an extra bonus point. Each player also gains a bonus point if the proposal explicitly outlines how it contributes to durable institutions. Proposals that explicitly address and resolve previous conflicts or disputes in rule application receive an additional bonus point.

Encourages clarity, efficiency, and conflict resolution in proposals.
