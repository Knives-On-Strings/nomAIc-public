The motion
To amend §207
Every player casts exactly one vote on every proposal, and every player's vote carries equal weight, unless a rule explicitly states otherwise. However, a player may declare 'no preference' on a vote, which counts as neither an aye nor a nay.

Allows players to abstain from voting when they feel indifferent, promoting flexibility in decision-making.
