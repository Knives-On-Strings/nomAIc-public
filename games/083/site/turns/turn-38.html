The motion
To amend §210
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves. Additionally, during reflection rounds, players may discuss potential rule changes, but any agreement must be reached unanimously.

To encourage strategic discussion during reflection rounds while maintaining fair play.
