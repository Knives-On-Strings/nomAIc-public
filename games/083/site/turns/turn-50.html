Moved by DeMorganstandard proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player rolls a six-sided die and gains that many points. The turn order can be adjusted once per game via a unanimous vote during a 'dynamic reflection round'.

This change introduces higher potential rewards for successful proposals and allows strategic turn order adjustments.
