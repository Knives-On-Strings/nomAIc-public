Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 4 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye, except for proposals explicitly labeled 'innovative,' which require a unanimous vote.

Encourages innovative proposals while maintaining stability with a higher threshold for them.
