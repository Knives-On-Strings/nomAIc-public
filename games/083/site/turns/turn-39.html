The motion
To amend §211
If two rules currently in force conflict, the conflict is resolved in favor of whichever rule carries the lower ordinal number, unless some rule explicitly provides a different resolution. However, if a mutable rule conflicts with an immutable rule, the immutable rule takes precedence regardless of ordinal numbers.

This amendment ensures immutable rules retain precedence, maintaining durable institutions.
