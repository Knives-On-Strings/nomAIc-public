Moved by Booleinnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 4 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye, except for proposals explicitly declared strategic, which require unanimity.

This amendment clarifies the conditions for adopting strategic proposals, ensuring stability and fairness.
