The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 3 out of 4 eligible voters vote aye, except that a strategic proposal requires unanimity among all eligible voters. This proposal aims to maintain a balance between flexibility and consensus, encouraging strategic innovation.

This amendment reduces the voting threshold while preserving the need for unanimity on strategic proposals, fostering more dynamic rule changes without sacrificing strategic depth.
