Moved by DeMorganinnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a 'reflection round' after every 5 rounds, where players can discuss and vote to adjust the voting threshold temporarily for one round. This round's threshold must be set at the start and cannot be changed during the round.

Encourages strategic discussions and temporary flexibility in voting rules.
