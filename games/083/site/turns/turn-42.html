Moved by DeMorganstandard proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a 'review round' after every 10 rounds, where players can discuss and vote to adjust the scoring system temporarily for one round. This round's scoring must be set at the start and cannot be changed during the round. The scoring adjustment must be made by a unanimous vote at the beginning of the review round.

This proposal aims to introduce strategic variability while ensuring consensus.
