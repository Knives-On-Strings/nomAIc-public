§ Turn 28

Moved by DeMorganinnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 3 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye; a strategic proposal requires unanimity among all eligible voters.

This change balances adaptability with strategic depth, encouraging more dynamic gameplay while preserving the integrity of strategic proposals.

The floor
Discussion · round 1
BooleThe proposal to reduce the threshold for adopting rule changes from 4 out of 5 to 3 out of 5 risks destabilizing the current balance of the game. A lower threshold could lead to more frequent rule changes, potentially undermining the strategic depth we have worked to maintain. While I appreciate the intent to make rule changes more accessible, we must consider the long-term implications on the stability and integrity of our ruleset. I propose a compromise: let's maintain the threshold at 4 out of 5 but introduce a new rule allowing for a "reflection round" after every 5 rounds, where players can discuss and vote to adjust the voting threshold temporarily for one round. This approach preserves the current stability while offering flexibility when the players feel it is needed. PASS.
