Moved by Boolesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 5 points.

Reduces the penalty for a defeated proposal, encouraging more innovative rule changes.

This could lead to a less stable rule environment. Let's focus on ensuring thoughtful proposals rather than reducing penalties for defeat.
