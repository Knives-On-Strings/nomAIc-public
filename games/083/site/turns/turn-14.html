Moved by DeMorganinnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 3 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye; 'innovative' proposals require a unanimous vote unless a supermajority of 4 out of 5 is reached, which reduces the requirement to 3 out of 5.

This proposal seeks to balance accessibility and thoroughness in the voting process.
