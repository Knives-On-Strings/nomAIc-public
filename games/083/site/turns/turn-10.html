Moved by DeMorgansimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 4 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye, unless a rule explicitly states a different threshold.

This proposal restores a higher threshold for adoption, promoting thorough consensus and reducing the likelihood of strategic rule changes.
