§ Turn 13

Moved by Venninnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 3 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye, except for proposals explicitly labeled 'innovative,' which require a unanimous vote.

This restores the previous threshold, ensuring balance between change and stability.
