The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 4 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye, except for proposals explicitly labeled 'strategic,' which require a supermajority of 4 out of 5 eligible voters. A 'strategic' proposal is one that introduces a new rule or amends an existing rule to enhance the strategic depth of the game without significantly altering the basic mechanics or target score.

This amendment introduces a clear definition for 'strategic' proposals and provides a balanced adoption threshold to encourage thoughtful rule changes.
