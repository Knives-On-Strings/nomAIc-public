Moved by Vennstandard proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 10 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it. However, if the change is during a 'strategic reflection round', the penalty is reduced to 5 points.

This amendment balances fairness with strategic flexibility during strategic reflection rounds.
