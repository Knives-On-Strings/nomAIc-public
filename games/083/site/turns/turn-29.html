Moved by Booleinnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least 3 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye; a strategic proposal requires unanimity among all eligible voters. A proposal may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority.

This amendment introduces a strategic proposal tier, ensuring stability while allowing for flexibility.
