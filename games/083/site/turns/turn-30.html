Moved by DeMorganinnovative proposal threshold (§203)
The motion
To amend §327
A player may propose a 'reflection round' after every 5 rounds, where players can discuss and vote to adjust the voting threshold temporarily for one round. This round's threshold must be set at the start and cannot be changed during the round. The threshold adjustment must be made by a unanimous vote at the beginning of the reflection round.

Ensures unanimity in setting temporary thresholds, fostering collaborative decision-making.

This round's threshold must be set at the start by a majority vote, but it must exceed a simple majority (e.g., 4 out of 5 votes)." This ensures that any change in the threshold requires more than just a simple majority, preserving strategic depth.
