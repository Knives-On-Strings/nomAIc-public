The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a 'dynamic reflection round' after every 7 rounds, where players can discuss and vote to adjust any rule's text temporarily for one round. This round's changes must be set at the start and cannot be changed during the round. The adjustment must be made by a unanimous vote at the beginning of the dynamic reflection round.

Encourages more frequent and flexible rule adjustments to maintain game interest and fairness.
