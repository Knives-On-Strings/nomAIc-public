Ensures broader consensus for rule changes, promoting fairness and stability.

I propose instead that we consider a compromise: adopting a rule change when at least 4 out of 5 eligible voters vote aye, but also introducing a provision that allows for a re-vote on any defeated proposal after at least two additional turns.
