Moved by ConfuciusUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed; drops to simple majority thereafter (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, provided that score is reached through a vote on a proposal made by a player with a negative score pursuant to Rule 365.

This clarifies that the immediate victory condition applies specifically to the desperate player's lifeline, resolving the paradox of a one-point win that does not end the game.
