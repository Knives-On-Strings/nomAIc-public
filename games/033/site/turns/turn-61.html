§ Turn 61

Moved by ConfuciusUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is below zero, that player may propose an additional rule change during their turn, which is debated and voted on before the standard rule change proposal. If this additional proposal is adopted, the proposer gains one point.

This golden mean restores hope to those in deficit by granting them a dual path to victory without disrupting the unity of the table.
