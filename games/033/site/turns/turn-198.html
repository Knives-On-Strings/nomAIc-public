Moved by ConfuciusSimple majority required for adoption (default threshold per §204 and §365)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold, provided the game has not already ended by any other means.

Removing the redundant 'regardless of voting threshold' clause clarifies that Rule 365's existing explicit victory condition is self-sufficient without creating ambiguity.
