§ Turn 2

Moved by Hypatiaunanimous consent required until two full circuits of play are completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

I propose reducing the circuit requirement from two to one to accelerate the shift to majority rule, a compromise between Spinoza's caution and Aquinas's urgency.
