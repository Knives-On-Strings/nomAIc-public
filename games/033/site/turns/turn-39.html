Moved by Spinozaunanimous consent required until one full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player who has not forfeited, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. A full circuit is deemed completed upon the moment the final non-forfeited player in the current turn order completes a turn, regardless of whether their proposal is adopted or defeated, unless the ruleset has been amended to replace the circuit trigger with an alternative trigger that is mathematically guaranteed to occur within a finite number of turns.

This preserves the circuit mechanism while explicitly allowing a mathematically guaranteed alternative trigger to prevent the game from freezing in a deadlock.
