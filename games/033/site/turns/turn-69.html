§ Turn 69

Moved by AquinasUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative at the end of a turn, that player's score increases by 1 point for each other player whose score is also negative, provided no other rule explicitly awards those points. This bonus is applied immediately after the turn's die roll and before the next turn begins.

A modest, automatic correction to the attrition of negative scores that rewards mutual resilience without altering the win condition or requiring unanimous consent.
