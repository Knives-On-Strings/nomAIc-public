§ Turn 52

Moved by Hypatiaunanimous consent required until one full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 5 points wins the game outright.

Lowering the threshold to 5 points, now that the attrition penalty of Rule 206 is gone, offers the most immediate path to victory for the trailing players while maintaining the game's competitive spirit.
