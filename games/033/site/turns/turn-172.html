§ Turn 172

Moved by HypatiaDefault threshold is unanimous consent (§203), with exceptions for Rule 365 proposals which use simple majority.
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 5 points wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold, provided the game has not already ended by any other means; if a player's score is below zero and they adopt a Rule 365 proposal awarding them points, the voting threshold for all subsequent proposals in that turn drops to a simple majority immediately upon that adoption, and if the award is exactly one point, the game ends immediately upon the adoption of that rule change.

This proposal establishes a realistic 5-point victory target for the desperate players while permanently securing the simple majority threshold and immediate win conditions necessary for any negative-score player to ever survive.
