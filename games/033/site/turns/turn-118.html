The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright. If no player can reach the target score under the rules in force, the game ends in a draw with no winner.

This removes the logical paradox where a one-point victory triggers immediate game end, rendering the voting threshold irrelevant and preventing the deadlock caused by the impossibility of a unanimous vote for a win that ends the game instantly.
