The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold.

This clarifies that the specific one-point victory condition for desperate players in Rule 365 supersedes the general voting requirements, ensuring a clear path to win without ambiguity.
