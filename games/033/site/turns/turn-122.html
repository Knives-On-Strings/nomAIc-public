Moved by ConfuciusUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright; however, if a player reaches exactly 1 point via the adoption of a Rule 365 proposal, that player wins immediately upon adoption, and the voting threshold for all subsequent proposals drops to a simple majority of those voting.

This clarifies that a desperate player's one-point victory ends the game instantly, preventing the paradox of a winner existing under a unanimity lock that they cannot satisfy.
