§ Turn 24

Moved by Spinozaunanimous consent required until one full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player who has not forfeited, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. A full circuit is deemed completed upon the moment the final non-forfeited player in the current turn order completes a turn, regardless of whether their proposal is adopted or defeated. However, no player may be required to complete a turn if doing so would render further play impossible under the current ruleset.

I amend the circuit definition to prevent the game from stalling if a rule change is needed to make play possible, ensuring the threshold drops only when a full circuit is genuinely achievable.
