Moved by AquinasUnanimous consent required until one full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §210
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves, and all players may discuss the game within the normal course of play, including during the debate phase of their own turn and the debate phase of any other player's turn.

Clarifying that open discussion is permitted during the debate phase removes the ambiguity that previously chilled necessary coordination.
