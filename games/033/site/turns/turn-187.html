§ Turn 187

Moved by HypatiaSimple majority required for adoption (default threshold per §204 and §365)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player with a score below zero who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold or any other rule provision, provided the game has not already ended by any other means.

This clarifies the immediate victory condition for negative-score players to eliminate ambiguity and prevent the game from stalling on procedural disputes.

The adopted proposal adds a new rule but does not trigger any existing rule that awards or deducts points based on the voting division or the adoption of a Rule 365 proposal (since the proposal was made by Hypatia, who has a negative score but did not use the Rule 365 mechanism to award herself points in this specific turn, nor did the proposal text itself award points to any player).
