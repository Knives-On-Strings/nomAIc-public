The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 10 points wins the game outright, unless all players have failed to reach a positive score by the end of the 200th turn, in which case the game ends in a draw.

This clarifies the win condition for the current low-score environment while preserving the game's integrity against perpetual deadlock.
