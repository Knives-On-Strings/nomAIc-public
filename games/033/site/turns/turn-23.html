The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 5 points, unless the proposer is the first to reach a target score of 100 points in the current turn sequence, in which case the loss is waived and the game continues.

To prevent the game from stalling in a loop of negative scores by sparing the proposer's points if they are moments from victory, thus honoring the common good of finishing the game.
