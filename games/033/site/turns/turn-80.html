§ Turn 80

Moved by HypatiaUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright. If a player reaches 1 point, the game ends immediately.

Explicitly clarifying that reaching the target ends the game immediately prevents any ambiguity about when the win condition triggers.

The floor
