§ Turn 117

Moved by ConfuciusUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, unless that player's score was negative pursuant to Rule 365 at the moment of adoption, in which case the game ends immediately upon that adoption regardless of the point total reached.

This harmonizes the victory condition with the desperate player's lifeline, ensuring that a Rule 365 adoption ending the game is recognized as a valid win condition even if the exact point threshold is not met by standard calculation.
