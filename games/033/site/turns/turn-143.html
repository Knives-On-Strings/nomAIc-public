The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold. If a player with a negative score adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point, that player wins immediately even if the proposal is voted on under a threshold other than simple majority.

To eliminate the remaining ambiguity regarding the victory condition for a -1 point adoption, ensuring the game ends instantly upon that specific event regardless of the current voting threshold.
