The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. If the proposal is defeated, the proposer loses 5 points.

Clarify that the penalty for defeat is a fixed loss of 5 points, removing ambiguity about whether the penalty applies if the ruleset changes mid-turn.
