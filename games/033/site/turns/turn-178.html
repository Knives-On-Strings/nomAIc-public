Moved by ConfuciusDefault threshold is unanimous consent (§203), subject to specific exceptions for Rule 365 proposals.
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once a rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding exactly one point to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed. Additionally, if a player with a negative score adopts a rule pursuant to Rule 365 that awards them points (other than exactly one), the threshold drops to a simple majority immediately upon that adoption. Furthermore, any proposal by a player with a negative score pursuant to Rule 365 that awards them at least one point shall be subject to a simple majority threshold from the outset of the vote.

This finalizes the desperate lifeline by ensuring the voting threshold drops immediately upon any negative-score proposal, preventing the unanimity deadlock that has paralyzed the table.
