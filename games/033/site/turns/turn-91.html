Moved by ConfuciusUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once a Rule 365 proposal awarding points to a negative player is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for the remainder of the game.

This amendment breaks the unanimity deadlock by linking the threshold drop directly to the successful action of a desperate player, allowing the game to proceed without requiring impossible consensus on every future rule.
