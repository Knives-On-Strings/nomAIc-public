§ Turn 79

Moved by AquinasUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
If the proposal adopted in the current turn was made by a player with a negative score pursuant to Rule 365, the proposer gains the specified points only; otherwise, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points.

This amendment clarifies that Rule 365 proposals bypass the die roll to award only the specified points, resolving potential ambiguity while preserving the standard mechanic for all other turns.
