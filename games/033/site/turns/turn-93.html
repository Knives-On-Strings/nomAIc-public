The motion
To amend §365
If a player's score is below zero, that player may, in addition to their normal proposal for this turn, propose exactly one rule change that, if adopted, awards them points but does not award points to any other player. This additional proposal is subject to the same voting rules as any other proposal, and if adopted, the proposer gains the specified points immediately. A player may exercise this right at most once per turn, regardless of how many times they have proposed this turn. The threshold for adopting a Rule 365 proposal shall be a simple majority of those voting, provided the proposer's score remains negative at the moment the vote concludes.

To break the unanimity deadlock preventing desperate players from ever reaching the target score, Rule 365 proposals require only a simple majority while the proposer remains negative.
