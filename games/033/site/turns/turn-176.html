§ Turn 176

Moved by AquinasDefault threshold is unanimous consent (§203), with exceptions for Rule 365 proposals which use simple majority.
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, provided the voting threshold for such adoption is a simple majority of eligible voters. The general target score for all other winning conditions shall be 5 points.

Establishing a clear 5-point threshold for general play prevents immediate deadlock while preserving the desperate lifeline mechanism.
