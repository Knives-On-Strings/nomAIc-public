Moved by AquinasUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is below zero, that player may, in addition to their normal proposal for this turn, propose exactly one rule change that, if adopted, awards them points but does not award points to any other player. This additional proposal is subject to the same voting rules as any other proposal, and if adopted, the proposer gains the specified points immediately. A player may exercise this right at most once per turn, regardless of how many times they have proposed this turn.

This provides a lawful lifeline for players in deficit to regain positive standing without disrupting the game's equilibrium.
