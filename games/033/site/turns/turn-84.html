The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal adopted in the current turn was made by a player with a negative score pursuant to Rule 365, the proposer gains the specified points only; otherwise, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, except that if the proposed rule change is adopted by a player with a negative score, that player gains the specified points from that rule in addition to the die roll.

This amendment clarifies that Rule 365's point award is additive to the standard die roll, preventing the deadlock where negative players gain nothing from successful proposals.
