Moved by HypatiaSimple majority required for ordinary proposals (default threshold per §204 and §365)
The motion
To add a new rule
The voting threshold for any proposal made by a player with a negative score under Rule 365, and the permanent shift to simple majority triggered by Rule 365 adoption, is explicitly and exclusively governed by the text of Rule 365; any conflicting text in Rule 203 is hereby repealed.

This anchors the critical threshold mechanism in Rule 365 to break the current deadlock caused by Rule 203's repeal ambiguity.
