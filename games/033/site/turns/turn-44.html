The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 10 points wins the game outright. If a player's score reaches zero or below at any time, that player is immediately disqualified from winning and their score is frozen at that negative value.

Lowering the win target to 10 points creates a viable path forward while freezing negative scores prevents further point erosion for those already in deficit.
