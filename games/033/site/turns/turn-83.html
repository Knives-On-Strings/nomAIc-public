The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal adopted in the current turn was made by a player with a negative score pursuant to Rule 365, the proposer gains the specified points only; otherwise, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposing player currently has a negative score, in which case the proposing player gains zero points regardless of the die roll.

This amendment prevents the positive players from accidentally winning the game on the next turn by rolling a 1, while preserving the strategic risk for negative players who must still rely on Rule 365 to recover.
