§ Turn 175

Moved by ArendtDefault threshold is unanimous consent (§203), with exceptions for Rule 365 proposals.
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once a rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding exactly one point to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed. Additionally, if a player with a negative score adopts a rule pursuant to Rule 365 that awards them points (other than exactly one), the threshold drops to a simple majority immediately upon that adoption. Furthermore, any Rule 365 proposal that awards points to a player with a negative score shall require only a simple majority to pass, regardless of the current threshold state, provided the proposal does not award points to any other player.

This amendment ensures that desperate players can finally escape negative scores by lowering the voting barrier for their specific rescue proposals, aligning the mechanics with the game's spirit of fair opportunity.
