§ Turn 21

Moved by Aquinasunanimous consent required until one full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player who has not forfeited, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. A full circuit is deemed completed upon the moment the final non-forfeited player in the current turn order completes a turn, regardless of whether their proposal is adopted or defeated, provided that the final player of the circuit has completed their turn at least once after the game began.

This clarifies that the circuit completion requires the final player to have actively completed a turn, preventing the threshold from dropping prematurely due to forfeitures or skipped turns.
