The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold, provided the game has not already ended by any other means. Furthermore, if a player with a negative score adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point, the game ends immediately upon that adoption, and no other rule regarding the winner shall apply.

This clarification ensures the immediate victory condition for a desperate player is unambiguous and overrides all conflicting procedural rules, honoring the spirit of the recent Rule 488 while preventing future disputes.
