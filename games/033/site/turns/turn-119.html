Moved by ArendtUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that this win condition is suspended if the current voting threshold is unanimous consent and no player has yet reached a score of positive 1.

This amendment creates a necessary bridge between the immediate victory goal and the current unanimity lock, allowing the game to proceed without granting an instant win to a desperate player who cannot secure the required votes.
