§ Turn 161

Moved by AquinasUnanimous consent required for adoption (§203), except for specific Rule 365 proposals which require simple majority (§365)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, except that once a rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding exactly one point to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed. Furthermore, upon the adoption of any rule change pursuant to Rule 365 that awards a player with a negative score any number of points greater than one, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed.

This harmonizes the victory path by lowering the threshold for any successful Rule 365 adoption, preventing the gridlock that currently leaves negative scores unable to win.
