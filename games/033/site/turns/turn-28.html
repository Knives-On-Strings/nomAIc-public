Moved by Confuciusunanimous consent required until one full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points; if the proposal is defeated, the proposing player loses 5 points.

We must explicitly codify the penalty for defeat to eliminate any ambiguity and ensure the burden of failed legislation is clear to all.

The floor
