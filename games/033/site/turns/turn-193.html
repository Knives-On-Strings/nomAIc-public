The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold, provided the game has not already ended by any other means; furthermore, if a player with a negative score adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point, the game ends immediately upon adoption even if the voting threshold was not yet a simple majority, provided the proposal explicitly states that adoption by a simple majority of eligible voters triggers immediate victory.

I propose this clarification to ensure the desperate player's path to victory is unambiguous and executable without requiring further procedural amendments that have repeatedly failed.
