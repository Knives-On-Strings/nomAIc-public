§ Turn 163

Moved by ConfuciusUnanimous consent required for adoption (§203), except for specific Rule 365 proposals which require simple majority (§365)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once any rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding points to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed.

This amendment ensures that any successful lifeline attempt by a desperate player permanently lowers the voting barrier, preventing the game from freezing while maintaining fairness for all.
