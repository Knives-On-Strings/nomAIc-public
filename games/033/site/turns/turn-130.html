The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright. However, if a player with a negative score adopts a rule pursuant to Rule 365 that awards them exactly one point, the game ends immediately upon the adoption of that rule change, and that player is declared the winner regardless of the target score rule.

Clarifying that a Rule 365 one-point award instantly triggers a win under the existing termination condition, resolving the deadlock between the score target and the immediate victory clause.
