§ Turn 164

Moved by SpinozaUnanimous consent required for adoption (§203), except for specific Rule 365 proposals which require simple majority (§365)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once a rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding exactly one point to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed; further, once a rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding any positive number of points to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals.

To break the deadlock for desperate players, the threshold must drop upon *any* successful Rule 365 adoption, not merely the one-point case, allowing us to escape the negative score trap.
