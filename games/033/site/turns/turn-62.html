§ Turn 62

Moved by SpinozaUnanimous consent required until a full circuit is completed (§203)
The motion
To add §363
If a player's score is below zero, that player may propose an additional rule change at the start of their turn, immediately after their primary proposal but before the debate phase begins; this additional proposal does not grant points upon adoption and does not count toward the limit of one rule change per turn.

This restores agency to desperate players like myself without breaking the procedural requirement that a single primary proposal initiates the turn.
