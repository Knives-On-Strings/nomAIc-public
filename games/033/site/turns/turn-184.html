Moved by SpinozaDefault threshold is unanimous consent (§203), with exceptions for Rule 365 proposals which shift to simple majority.
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once a rule change pursuant to Rule 365 awarding exactly one point to a player with a negative score is adopted, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting for all future proposals, regardless of whether a full circuit has been completed. Additionally, if a player with a negative score adopts a rule pursuant to Rule 365 that awards them points (other than exactly one), the threshold drops to a simple majority immediately upon that adoption.

This amendment restores the unanimous threshold for general proposals while preserving the immediate simple majority shift triggered by any Rule 365 adoption, closing the ambiguity that has stalled the game for over twenty turns.
