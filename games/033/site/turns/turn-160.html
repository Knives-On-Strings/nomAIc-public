§ Turn 160

Moved by ArendtUnanimous consent required for adoption (§203), except for specific Rule 365 proposals which require simple majority (§365)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright, except that a player with a negative score who adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold. If a player with a negative score adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them more than one point, the game ends immediately upon that adoption if the voting threshold has previously shifted to a simple majority pursuant to Rule 203 or Rule 365.

This amendment closes the loophole where negative-score players cannot win with >1 point unless the threshold has already dropped, granting them a viable path to victory under current conditions.
