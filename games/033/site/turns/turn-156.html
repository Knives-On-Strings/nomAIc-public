The motion
To amend §208

However, if a player with a negative score adopts a Rule 365 proposal awarding them exactly one point, that player wins the game immediately upon that adoption, regardless of the voting threshold, and this victory condition takes precedence over the general target score.

To explicitly codify the immediate victory for the desperate while preserving the existing target score for all others.
