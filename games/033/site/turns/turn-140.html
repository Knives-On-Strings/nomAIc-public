The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 28 points wins the game outright, provided that a player with a negative score pursuant to Rule 365 may adopt a rule awarding them exactly one point to win immediately upon such adoption, which shall supersede the target score of 28.

This anchors the win condition at 28 to honor Arendt's proposal while preserving the desperate player's immediate victory path under Rule 365.
