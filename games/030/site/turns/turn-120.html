The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is amended: Upon adoption of a rule change, a player who voted against it gains one point and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

We raise the floor to one point to ensure structural resilience and satisfy Rule 114 while neutralizing the coercion that threatens our ability to play.
