The motion
To amend §204
A player who voted nay on an adopted change gains 5 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

I propose reducing the dissent penalty from 10 to 5 points to break the deadlock with Hobbes while maintaining the safety valve he demands.
