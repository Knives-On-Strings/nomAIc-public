Moved by Kantsimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. This threshold, once lowered, may never be raised by any subsequent rule change.

We must legally lock in the majority threshold to prevent opponents from exploiting rule 114 to force a return to unanimity.
