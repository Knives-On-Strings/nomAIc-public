The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is amended: A player who voted against an adopted change gains fifteen points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

Elevating the penalty from ten to fifteen points restores sufficient friction to deter obstructionist voting without rendering the game unwinnable.
