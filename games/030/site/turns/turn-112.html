Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §204
Upon adoption of a rule change, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. A player who voted against an adopted change gains one point and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

I restore the penalty to one point to preserve the necessary coercion that breaks unanimous deadlock, ensuring the game remains playable without violating the structural integrity of Rule 114.
