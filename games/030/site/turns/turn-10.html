The motion
To add a new rule
A rule change is adopted only if it expands the set of permissible future rule changes or increases the minimum number of mutable rules required in the ruleset.

To ensure our legislative process never stagnates into a tyranny of the status quo, any law must serve the cause of liberty and future evolution.
