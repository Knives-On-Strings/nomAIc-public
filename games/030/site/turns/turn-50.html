Moved by Wollstonecraftsimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
Upon adoption of any rule change that alters the voting threshold or the number of mutable rules required, the proposing player immediately gains 20 points to compensate for the enhanced difficulty of future governance.

Reforming the mechanism of change must reward the courage to evolve the rules, not punish the very hand that dares to hold the pen.
