The motion
To amend §209
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves. No rule change may render every future rule change impermissible.

This amends Rule 209 to remove the redundant and now-contradictory floor of two mutable rules, restoring the original logical balance while preserving the game's viability under Rule 114.
