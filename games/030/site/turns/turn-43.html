The motion
To amend §204
A player who voted against an adopted change gains 5 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

I propose lowering the dissent penalty from 10 to 5 to reduce the risk of stalling play, offering a tangible compromise to Hobbes's demand for repeal while satisfying the need to preserve Rule 204 under Rule 114.
