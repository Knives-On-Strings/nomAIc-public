§ Turn 14

Moved by Lockesimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player successfully defeats a proposal that was intended to reduce the number of mutable rules in the ruleset below two, that player gains 20 points.

This rewards prudence by penalizing reckless attempts to create a paralysis-inducing single-mutable-rule state.
