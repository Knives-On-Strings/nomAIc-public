The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is amended: The penalty for a player who voted nay on an adopted rule change under rule 203 is fifteen points.

I must raise the penalty to fifteen points to effectively deter obstruction and secure my victory, as lower penalties have proven insufficient.
