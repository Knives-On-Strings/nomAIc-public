The motion
To add §345
Rule 204 is amended: A player who votes against an adopted rule change gains 5 points and is bound by that change exactly as if they had voted for it.

I propose capping the dissent penalty at 5 points to mitigate the severe score drain of Rule 204 while preserving its binding nature, a compromise Hobbes and Wollstonecraft have previously found acceptable.
