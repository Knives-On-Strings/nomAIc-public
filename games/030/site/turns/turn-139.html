The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is amended: A player who voted against an adopted change gains no points; this amendment stands until repealed by a proposal that also specifies the exact number of points to be gained upon such dissent.

I offer a reversible compromise that removes the penalty immediately while preserving the option to restore it, satisfying Rousseau's demand for freedom and Hobbes's need for a potential deterrent.
