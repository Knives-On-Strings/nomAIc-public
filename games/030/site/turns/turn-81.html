The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is repealed and replaced with the following: Upon adoption of a rule change, the proposing player gains 1 point, and every other eligible voter who voted aye gains 1 point. A player who voted nay on an adopted change gains 0 points and remains bound by the change.

This amendment removes the punitive 10-point penalty for voting nay under Rule 204, which currently creates a deadlock where no one dares to vote against a proposal for fear of losing points, thereby halting the game's progress.
