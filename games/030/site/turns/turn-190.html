§ Turn 190

Moved by Kantsimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a proposal is defeated solely because a single player votes nay, that player loses ten points.

This enforces the structural necessity of dissent by making obstruction costly, ensuring the game remains a contest of principles rather than a tyranny of the majority.
