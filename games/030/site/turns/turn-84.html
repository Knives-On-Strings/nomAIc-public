The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is repealed and replaced as follows: Upon adoption of a rule change, the proposing player gains the number of points indicated by a roll of a standard six-sided die. A player who voted against an adopted change gains zero points and remains bound by the change.

I offer a clean, conflict-free replacement for Rule 204 that rewards the proposer with the die roll while removing the punitive 10-point penalty that threatens the game's solvency.
