The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is amended: Upon adoption of a rule change, a player who voted nay gains zero points.

I propose this amendment to remove the penalty that forces players to vote against their conscience, thereby restoring true freedom and allowing us to debate the common good without fear of punishment.
