The motion
To add a new rule
If a player votes nay on a rule change that is subsequently adopted, that player's score is adjusted by +5 points for this vote only, and they gain no further penalty from Rule 204 for the remainder of the game.

I offer a concrete compromise to soften the harshness of Rule 204 while maintaining the stability of Rule 319, ensuring no player faces ruin from a single dissent.
