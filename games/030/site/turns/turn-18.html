Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §209
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves. No player may propose a rule change that reduces the maximum number of mutable rules permitted by rule 209 to fewer than one. The minimum number of mutable rules in force shall never be less than one.

We must anchor the game in a single mutable rule to guarantee future possibility, rejecting Hobbes and Locke's attempts to freeze our institutions at an arbitrary floor of two.
