The motion
To add §379
Rule 204 is repealed. Upon adoption of a rule change, the proposer gains 10 points and no other player gains or loses points.

I offer a compromise that removes the fatal penalty conflict of Rule 204 while satisfying Rule 114 by leaving Rule 202 fully operative, allowing play to continue without further deadlock.
