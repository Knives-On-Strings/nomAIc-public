Moved by Kantsimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player who proposes a rule change that is defeated (under Rule 206) gains 5 points, and the turn ends immediately.

Compensating for the loss incurred by a failed proposal incentivizes principled voting over point-maximizing paralysis.
