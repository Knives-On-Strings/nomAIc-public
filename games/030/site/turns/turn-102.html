Moved by Lockesimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §204
A player who voted against an adopted change gains 1 point and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

I propose reducing the penalty from 10 to 1 to remove the coercive tyranny that forces players to vote aye, while retaining a minimal cost to preserve the rule's existence under Rule 114.
