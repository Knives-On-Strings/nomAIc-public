The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is repealed. Upon the adoption of a rule change, a player who voted against it gains ten points.

By repealing Rule 204 and restating its core penalty in a new rule, I create a loophole that allows future rule changes to remove the penalty without violating the structural floor of two mutable rules mandated by Rule 209.
