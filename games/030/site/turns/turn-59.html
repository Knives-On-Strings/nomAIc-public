§ Turn 59

Moved by Hobbessimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. If the proposal is defeated, the proposing player gains zero points and their turn ends.

This amendment neutralizes the -10 point penalty for failed proposals under Rule 206, stabilizing the score mechanics and ensuring Rule 114 remains valid without risking total stasis.
