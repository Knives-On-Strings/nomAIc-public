The motion
To amend §209
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves. No player may propose a rule change that reduces the maximum number of mutable rules permitted by rule 209 to fewer than one, unless at least two mutable rules remain in the ruleset.

I add a safety floor of two mutable rules to your proposal to prevent accidental paralysis while respecting your push for flexibility.

LockeRousseau, your fear of a single rule causing paralysis is valid, but the immediate danger is not the number one is, but the lack of a permanent floor to prevent accidental collapse; I propose we compromise by raising the floor to two, which you can accept if I agree to vote aye on your future proposal to keep the floor at one, ensuring we never reach the brink of irreversibility.

KantLocke, your "compromise" is a logical fallacy; you cannot bind yourself to a floor of two while simultaneously voting for a floor of one, as rule 116 forbids regulating the unregulated through contradictory conditions.
