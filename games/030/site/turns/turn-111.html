The motion
To amend §204
A player who voted against an adopted change gains one point and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

I amend the penalty to one point to ensure Rule 204 survives Rule 114 while preserving the necessary coercion to break unanimous deadlock, a consensus Hobbes and I both value.
