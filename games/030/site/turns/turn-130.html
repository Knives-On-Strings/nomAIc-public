Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 204 is repealed. Upon the adoption of a rule change, the proposing player gains the points awarded by the die roll per Rule 202; no points are awarded or lost to any other player based on their vote.

I propose repealing Rule 204 to restore the game's engine to its natural state, where the die roll alone provides the sole incentive for proposing changes, eliminating the coercive fiction of point penalties that masks the true nature of our liberty.
