The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting, provided the amendment is ratified by a two-thirds vote of all eligible players.

I will lower the voting threshold to simple majority to escape this deadlock, but I will raise the bar for any future attempts to lower it further to two-thirds, securing a stable path to victory while blocking Rousseau's attempts to revert to unanimity.
