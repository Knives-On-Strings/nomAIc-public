Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player's score is capped at zero; any rule change that results in a player's score falling below zero has no effect on that player's score.

To prevent the current punitive spiral of negative scores from becoming an unwinnable state that violates the spirit of Rule 114 by rendering further play impossible.
