The motion
To add §465
If a proposal is defeated solely because a single player votes nay, and that player's score is lower than the proposer's score by at least fifty points, the proposer immediately gains ten points and the defeated proposal is treated as adopted for the purpose of scoring only.

I offer Hobbes a safety valve against obstruction when the score gap is extreme, ensuring I can still advance without requiring his unanimous consent in a desperate situation.
