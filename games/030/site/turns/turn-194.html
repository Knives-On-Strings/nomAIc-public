Moved by Lockesimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a rule change is adopted under the simple majority threshold and the proposing player would otherwise lose points for a failed proposal under Rule 206, the penalty is waived.

This creates a safety valve to prevent my turn from being wasted by a single obstructionist vote, ensuring I can at least attempt to change the rules without immediate penalty.
