The motion
To add §302
When a rule change is adopted under the simple majority threshold established by rule 203, any player who voted against it may propose one additional rule change in the immediately following turn, free of the usual proposal cost, to ensure that dissenting voices remain active in shaping the laws they are bound to.

This amendment converts my prior defeat into a strategic advantage by letting me immediately shape the rules again, while preventing the game from stalling on a single player's dissent.
