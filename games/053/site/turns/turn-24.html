§ Turn 24

Moved by Kantsimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. This threshold shift may never be repealed or delayed by any subsequent rule change.

I must enforce the two-circuit requirement to prevent the collapse of Rule 213 that a lower threshold would invite, ensuring the mutable cap can be maintained without triggering a stalemate.
