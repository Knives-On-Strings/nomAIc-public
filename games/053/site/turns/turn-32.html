§ Turn 32

Moved by Lockesimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add §331
If a player's score is zero or less at the start of their turn, that player must forfeit the game immediately.

This clarifies the forfeiture trigger to include zero, preventing the zero-point deadlock that currently risks violating Rule 114.
