§ Turn 3

Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add §302
No rule change may be adopted that renders the game unwinnable by any player, nor may any rule be amended to allow a player to gain points without completing a turn.

I propose this guardrail to prevent Hobbes or others from manipulating the rules into a state where victory is impossible, which would only serve the selfish interests of the manipulator rather than the common good.

Rule 202 awards points only upon adoption of a proposal, but the proposal text itself does not contain the awarding mechanism; therefore, no points are awarded or deducted, and no rules are surgically amended.
