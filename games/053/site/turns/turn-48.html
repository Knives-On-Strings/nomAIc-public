The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 203 is hereby amended to require unanimous consent for any proposal that alters the voting threshold or the conditions for its own adoption.

Protecting the unanimity mechanism from being eroded by majority vote prevents the permanent deadlock that violates Rule 114.
