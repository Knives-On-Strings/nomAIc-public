The motion
To add a new rule
A player whose score is negative at the start of their turn may propose exactly one rule change without penalty; if adopted, they gain points as if starting from zero. If defeated, their turn ends with no points gained or lost. This rule overrides Rule 305 for the specific purpose of proposing and voting on rule changes.

This allows me to remain in the game and propose rules to fix my negative status, preventing an immediate loss under Rule 305 while respecting the intent of Rule 114 to keep mutable rules alive.
