The motion
To amend §319
Rule 213 is hereby amended to state: "If a player's score is negative at the start of their turn, that player must forfeit the game immediately."

Clarifying Rule 305's immediate forfeiture within the stalemate clause prevents the paradox of a forfeited player continuing to obstruct the mutable rule count.
