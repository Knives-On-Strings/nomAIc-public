The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative at the start of their turn, that player's turn ends immediately with no points gained or lost, and they must propose a rule change that sets a new target score of at least 50 points; failure to propose such a change within the same turn results in immediate forfeiture and loss of all points.

This clears my current deficit immediately by binding the penalty to a constructive act, restoring the game's flow while ensuring I cannot simply pass turns to wait out the clock.
