§ Turn 19

Moved by Hobbessimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 213 is hereby amended to state: "If a player's score is negative at the start of their turn, that player must forfeit the game immediately."

I must explicitly remove the right to continue playing while in debt, preventing any future proposals from exploiting a loophole to keep Locke in the game.
