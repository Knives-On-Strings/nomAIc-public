Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses no points unless specifically required by another rule.

We must restore the dignity of failure so that a lost turn does not punish a player for the collective failure to agree.
