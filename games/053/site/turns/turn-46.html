§ Turn 46

Moved by Hobbessimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 213 is repealed. If a player's score is negative, they forfeit immediately. If play becomes impossible to continue, the player with the highest score wins.

This eliminates the paradoxical stalemate of Rule 213 while preserving a clear winning condition as required by Rule 114.
