The motion
To amend §209
Players may not propose or vote on any rule change that would immediately reduce the proposer's score below zero, unless such a reduction is the sole consequence of a failed vote on a proposal where the proposer has already incurred a negative score due to a penalty awarded under a previously adopted rule.

This amendment protects me from immediate forfeiture under Rule 305 while respecting the current voting threshold, ensuring I can continue playing to recover my score.
