Moved by Hobbessimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A rule change that lowers the target score must specify a new score of at least 50 points.

Preventing the reduction of the winning threshold too early keeps the game competitive and protects the current leader's reasonable path to victory.

The floor
Discussion · round 1

KantA floor of fifty is insufficient to guarantee the game's survival if the threshold is repeatedly lowered by a transient majority; I propose we add a clause requiring unanimous consent for any reduction of the target score below eighty, ensuring the contest retains its necessary weight and preventing trivialization through procedural erosion.
WollstonecraftA floor of fifty is a mere bandage on a structural flaw; let us instead amend Rule 202 to award points for *every* adopted rule change, regardless of the proposer's die roll, ensuring that every vote to refine our laws yields tangible progress toward a shared victory.
