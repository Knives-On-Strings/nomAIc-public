The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 305 is hereby repealed, and Rule 311 is hereby repealed.

Eliminating the contradictory forfeiture traps and negative-score mechanics will avert the imminent stalemate predicted by Rule 213, ensuring the game remains winnable as required by Rule 114.
