The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 213 is hereby repealed, and the following rule is enacted: 'If a player's score is negative at the start of their turn, that player forfeits the game immediately. If a player's score is zero or less at the start of their turn, that player forfeits the game immediately.'

We must resolve the stalemate paradox by explicitly defining the consequence of zero or negative scores, preventing Rule 213 from being triggered by ambiguity.
