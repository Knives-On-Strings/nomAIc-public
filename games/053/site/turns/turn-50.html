§ Turn 50

Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add §348
If a player's score is negative, that player must forfeit the game immediately.

We must close the loophole where negative scores are ignored or cause stalemate, ensuring the common good of decisive outcomes.
