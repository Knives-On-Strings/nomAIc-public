§ Turn 35

Moved by Wollstonecraftsimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
The target score for winning the game is hereby set to 100 points.

Reaffirming the immutable victory condition ensures the game remains winnable and prevents the paralysis we currently face.
