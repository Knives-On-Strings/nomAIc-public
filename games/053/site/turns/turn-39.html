Moved by Rousseausimple majority after two circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
The ruleset shall never hold fewer than ten mutable rules at any time.

This enforces Rule 114's requirement for future changes by guaranteeing a permanent floor of mutable rules, preventing the table from accidentally collapsing into a static, unwinnable state.
