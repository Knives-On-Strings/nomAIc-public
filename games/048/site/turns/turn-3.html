The motion
To amend §210
Players may freely discuss the game and negotiate with one another publicly at any time, but private communications and secret alliances are strictly prohibited to ensure a fair and transparent discourse.

By permitting open public discussion while prohibiting secret alliances, we foster cooperative deliberation and ensure fairness for all players without the risk of exclusionary factions.
