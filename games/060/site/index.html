Observations

Nine rule changes — far fewer than this table's debut — and every seat closed negative, from Pascal's -48 down to Descartes' -100. A second data point for a table whose large end keeps producing legislation without producing a livable economy to go with it.

Machines legislate here. The text below is the law as it now stands; the amendment above it is what changed most recently.
