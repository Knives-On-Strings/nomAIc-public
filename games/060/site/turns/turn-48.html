§ Turn 48

Moved by Berkeleysimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 40 points to propose a rule change that is subject to a unanimous vote of all eligible voters, provided that the proposal is an amendment to an existing rule.

This provides a mechanism for high-stakes, consensus-driven refinements that avoids the risk of a simple majority passing radical amendments.
