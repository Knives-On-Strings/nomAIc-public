The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a four-fifths supermajority; once turn order has carried play through two full circuits, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

I propose a tiered transition to a supermajority to bridge the gap between Leibniz's stability and Berkeley's need for progress.
