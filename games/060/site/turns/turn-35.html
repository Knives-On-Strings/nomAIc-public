The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 30 points to nominate a specific rule for immediate transmutation from mutable to immutable, provided the transmutation is passed by a unanimous vote of all eligible voters.

This provides a high-cost, high-consensus mechanism to protect vital legislative achievements from being undone by simple majorities.
