Moved by Leibnizsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 25 points to repeal any single mutable rule, provided the repeal is passed by a unanimous vote of all eligible voters.

This provides a high-cost, high-consensus mechanism for removing rules that have become detrimental to the stability of the institution.
