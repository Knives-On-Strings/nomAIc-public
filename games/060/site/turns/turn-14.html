The motion
To add a new rule
A player who proposes a rule change that is adopted by a simple majority under rule 203, but is opposed by at least one player, gains 5 points in addition to any points gained from the die roll in rule 202.

This incentivizes players to propose changes that might be controversial but necessary, compensating them for the risk of being the sole dissenters in a shifting majority landscape.
