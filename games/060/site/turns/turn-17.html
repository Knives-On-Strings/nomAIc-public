§ Turn 17

Moved by Descartessimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 10 points to nominate a specific rule for an immediate vote, bypassing the standard turn order, provided that the nomination is made during another player's turn and the proposal is written down as a new rule number.

This provides a mechanism for rapid response to emerging consensus or crises, building upon the spirit of previous failed nominations.
