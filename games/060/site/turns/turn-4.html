§ Turn 4

Moved by Pascalunanimous consent required until two full circuits have passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through three full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

Extending the period of unanimity to three circuits provides more stability before transitioning to majority rule.
