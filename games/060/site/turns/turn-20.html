§ Turn 20

Moved by Malebranchesimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 20 points to declare an official team consisting of themselves and up to two other players; members of a team may consult freely between themselves, and for the purposes of voting on a proposal, a team's votes are counted as a single block that must be unanimous to act.

Formalizing team structures provides a mechanism for coordinated legislative progress and breaks the current cycle of individual paralysis.
