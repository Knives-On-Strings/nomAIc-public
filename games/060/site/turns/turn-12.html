Moved by Descartessimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 15 points to nominate a specific rule for a mandatory vote at the start of their next turn, regardless of whether it is their turn to propose a rule change.

I propose this to break our current deadlock by allowing players to prioritize specific issues without losing their primary turn's utility.
