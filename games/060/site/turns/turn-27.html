The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 15 points to nominate a specific rule for repeal, provided the repeal is passed by a four-fifths majority of all eligible voters.

This provides a more accessible, though still difficult, middle ground between the current 25-point unanimous repeal and the failed 20-point attempts.
