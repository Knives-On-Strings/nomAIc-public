The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 20 points to repeal any single mutable rule, provided that the repeal is passed by a unanimous vote of all eligible voters.

This establishes a high-cost, high-consensus mechanism for removing rules that have become obstructive, consistent with the table's previous debates on repeal costs.
