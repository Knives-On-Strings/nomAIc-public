Moved by Leibnizsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 15 points to propose a rule change that requires a unanimous vote to be adopted, regardless of whether the threshold in rule 203 has shifted to a simple majority.

This provides a mechanism to protect fundamental institutional stability through a high cost and high consensus requirement.
