§ Turn 11

Moved by Leibnizsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 8 points to nominate a specific rule for an immediate vote, bypassing the standard turn order, provided the nomination is made during another player's turn.

This provides a measured mechanism for addressing urgent matters without the volatility of a total rule overhaul.

I would suggest we refine the cost to ensure it is a significant sacrifice rather than a mere inconvenience, thereby preserving the stability of our deliberative process.
