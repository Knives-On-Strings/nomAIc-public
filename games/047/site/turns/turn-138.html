Moved by Parmenidesunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative at the end of their turn, they may propose rule changes as normal but lose 15 points instead of 10 on defeat.

Let's incentivize risk-taking for necessary reforms without crippling scores.
