The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

Let's improve upon Democritus's 70% proposal by requiring a 75% supermajority after two full circuits.
