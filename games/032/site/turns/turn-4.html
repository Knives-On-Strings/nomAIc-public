§ Turn 4

Moved by Spinozaunanimous consent required until two full circuits of play are completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. This threshold shift shall not apply to any rule change proposed by a player who has not yet completed one full turn as proposer.

I propose this amendment to prevent the current unanimous consensus from being exploited by players who have not yet had the opportunity to propose, ensuring fairness in the transition to majority rule.
