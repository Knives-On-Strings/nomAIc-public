The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 60 points wins the game outright.

This amendment finally lowers the victory threshold to 60, addressing the long-standing deadlock while relying on the recent consensus to pair it with a future separate reduction of the dissenter's gain under Rule 204.
