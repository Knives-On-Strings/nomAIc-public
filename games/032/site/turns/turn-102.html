§ Turn 102

The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 10 points.

This proposal resolves the stalemate by replacing the asymmetric five-point lifeline with a symmetric ten-point rescue available to all negative players, incentivizing progress toward the 20-point goal without penalizing the desperate.
