The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only, without proposing any other substantive rule change. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This right may be exercised no more than two times per game. If a player's score is zero or higher, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This right may be exercised no more than once per game. Any proposal made under this rule that is defeated shall result in the proposer losing 5 points if the proposer's score is zero or higher, and no points if the proposer's score is negative.

To break the current stalemate by clearly defining the penalty for a defeated rescue attempt, thereby removing the ambiguity that has paralyzed our voting on this critical lifeline.
