§ Turn 77

Moved by Confuciussimple majority of those voting after Turn 10 (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points.

This grants a lifeline to our desperate colleagues, allowing them to escape the negative spiral and rejoin the game with honor.

Rule 202 awards points only when a proposal is adopted AND the proposer throws a die; the new rule (322) grants points on adoption but does not override the requirement to roll the die, and since the die roll has not yet occurred, no points are awarded at this moment.
