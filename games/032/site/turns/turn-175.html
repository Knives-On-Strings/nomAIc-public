The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 214 (mutable): If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only, without proposing any other substantive rule change. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This right may be exercised no more than once per game by any player whose score is negative. If a player's score is zero or higher, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This right may be exercised no more than once per game. The limit of one use for negative-score players is hereby declared mutable.

To break the deadlock and offer a fair, symmetrical path to victory for all players, I propose establishing a single, uniform rule that grants every player one chance to gain 5 points via a standalone proposal, regardless of score.
