The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 60 points wins the game outright, provided that Rule 206 is amended to state that a defeated proposal results in the loss of 2 points unless the proposer is acting under Rule 318, in which case no points are lost.

This unified amendment restores hope for deficit players while correcting the excessive penalty that currently deters necessary rule changes.
