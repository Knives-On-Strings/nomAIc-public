§ Turn 92

Moved by ArendtUnanimous consent required until Turn 10; currently Turn 92, but Rule 203 states threshold drops to simple majority only after two full circuits (conclusion of Turn 10). Since Turn 92 > 10, the threshold is simple majority.
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score reaches zero or higher, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to the penalty of defeat under Rule 206 if the proposal is defeated solely by the votes of players who are not currently losing points, and if adopted, the proposer gains 5 points.

This extends the lifeline of Rule 377 to players who have recovered from the death spiral, ensuring no one is permanently barred from the rescue mechanism.
