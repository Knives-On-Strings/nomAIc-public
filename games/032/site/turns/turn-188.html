§ Turn 188

Moved by Spinozasimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To repeal §203
and strike it from the statute.

Repealing the unanimity-to-majority transition rule eliminates the paradox of a win condition that can be lowered by a simple majority while opponents retain the power to raise it by unanimous vote, restoring a stable, predictable path to victory.
