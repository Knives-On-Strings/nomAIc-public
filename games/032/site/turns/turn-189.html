Moved by Arendtsimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting; for the purposes of this rule, two full circuits of every player occurs precisely at the conclusion of Turn 10, provided that Rule 208 is not currently mutable.

This creates a conditional threshold shift that breaks the deadlock by allowing a majority to fix the win condition to a reachable score without requiring impossible unanimity on a mutable target.
