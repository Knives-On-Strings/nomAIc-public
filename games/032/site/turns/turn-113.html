The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 10 points. This right may be exercised no more than three times per game.

Building on the recent unanimous cap of three uses, this amendment finalizes the compromise by raising the rescue value to 10 points to match the table's stated intent.
