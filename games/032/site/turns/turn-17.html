The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 2 points.

Reducing the penalty from 5 to 2 points encourages experimentation while still deterring frivolous proposals, and aligns with the consensus on proportional consequences.
