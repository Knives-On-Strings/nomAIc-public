§ Turn 38

Moved by Spinozasimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 2 points.

Aligns the defeat penalty with the newly established 2-point floor for dissenters to restore procedural symmetry and prevent point starvation.
