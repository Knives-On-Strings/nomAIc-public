The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. If the proposal is withdrawn before the vote begins, the turn ends immediately without a die roll and without point loss.

To prevent the stagnation of our scores and honor the new right of withdrawal, we must explicitly exempt withdrawn proposals from point penalties.
