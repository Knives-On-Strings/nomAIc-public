§ Turn 69

Moved by Arendtsimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To amend §321
If a proposal is withdrawn under Rule 307, the proposer's turn ends immediately and no points are gained or lost. If a proposal is defeated under Rule 206, the proposer loses 5 points, unless the proposer's score is negative, in which case the proposer loses no points.

Extending immunity from the five-point defeat penalty to all negative-scored players breaks the death spiral without altering the target score.
