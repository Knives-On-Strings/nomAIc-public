§ Turn 87

Moved by Arendtsimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 15 points wins the game outright.

Lowering the victory target to 15 points provides the most immediate and equitable path for all five desperate players to escape the negative spiral.
