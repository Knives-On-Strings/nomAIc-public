The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 208 (mutable): The first player to reach a target score of 2 points wins the game outright.

This sets a reachable target for all players immediately under the current simple majority threshold, ending the paralysis caused by the impossibility of the 1-point target for those with negative scores.
