The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is below -40, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to the penalty of defeat under Rule 206 if the proposal is defeated solely by the votes of players who are currently at a score of -40 or higher.

This targeted safety valve addresses the specific desperation of players in the -40s while preserving the game's stability for those near the finish line.
