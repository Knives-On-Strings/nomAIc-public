The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. However, this privilege may be exercised at most once per game by any single player.

A single-use cap on the lifeline balances the desperate's hope with the table's need for fairness, offering a final path to victory without inviting endless exploitation.
