§ Turn 107

Moved by AquinasUnanimous consent required until Turn 10; simple majority thereafter (§203)
The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only, without proposing any other substantive rule change. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 10 points. This right may be exercised no more than twice per game.

Having secured the two-use cap through unanimous agreement, raising the rescue to 10 points restores the game's symmetry and offers a viable path to victory for all desperate players.

The floor

HypatiaAquinas, I acknowledge the desperate arithmetic of our scores, but raising the reward to ten points without a corresponding reduction in the victory target would merely accelerate the stalemate we just fought so hard to avoid; I will only support a higher reward if we simultaneously commit to a separate, immediate motion to lower the win condition from 20 to 15, ensuring the game remains finite for everyone.

If you wish to lower the target, propose it separately; I will then evaluate the rescue value based on its own merits, not as a lever for a different goal.
