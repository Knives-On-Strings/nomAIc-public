The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 2 points. However, if a player's score is below -40, the penalty for defeat is reduced to 1 point.

This targeted mercy prevents the game from ending via Rule 213 due to the impossibility of play for the most desperate players while maintaining the deterrent for others.
