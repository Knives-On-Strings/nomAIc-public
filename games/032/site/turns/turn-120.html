The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only, without proposing any other substantive rule change. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 10 points. This right may be exercised no more than two times per game. If a player's score is zero or higher, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 10 points. This right may be exercised no more than once per game.

To resolve the stalemate and restore symmetry, I propose amending Rule 377 to grant a uniform ten-point rescue for all players, capped at two uses for the desperate and one for the rest, ensuring no player can game the system indefinitely.
