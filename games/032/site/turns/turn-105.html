The motion
To add a new rule
If a proposal to cap Rule 377 at exactly two uses is adopted, the proposer of that cap immediately gains 5 points.

This decouples the structural limit on the lifeline from the reward mechanism, allowing the table to agree on the necessary cap without fearing an asymmetrical point gain that previously doomed such proposals.
