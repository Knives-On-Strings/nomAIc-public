Moved by Aquinassimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This rule may be invoked at most once per player per game.

I propose a one-time cap on the lifeline to resolve the stalemate and secure the game's future.
