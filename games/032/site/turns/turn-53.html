Moved by Spinozasimple majority of those voting after Turn 10 (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 30 points wins the game outright.

Given the scores are deeply negative, only a drastically lowered victory threshold creates a mathematically reachable path for the desperate players to avoid permanent deadlock.
