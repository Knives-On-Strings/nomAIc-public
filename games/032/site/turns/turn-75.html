The motion
To amend §307
A proposal may be withdrawn by its proposer at any time before the vote begins, at which point the proposal is recorded as withdrawn rather than defeated, the proposer loses no points, the proposer's turn ends immediately, and no new proposal may be made by that proposer in the current turn.

This prevents the stalling tactic of repeated withdrawals that has consumed the last fifty turns while preserving the escape valve for the desperate.
