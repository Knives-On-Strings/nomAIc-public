Moved by Hypatiaunanimous consent required until two full circuits of play are completed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once the score of any single player reaches 50 points, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

This creates a winnable path to majority rule before the slow turn-based circuits are complete, rewarding early point accumulation while preserving unanimity until the game intensifies.
