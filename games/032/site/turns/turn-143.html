The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only, without proposing any other substantive rule change. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This right may be exercised no more than twice per game. If a player's score is zero or higher, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points. This right may be exercised no more than once per game. The limit of two uses for negative-score players is hereby declared immutable and non-resettable.

To break the century-long stalemate, we must finally secure the desperate player's lifeline as an unchangeable truth, allowing the game to proceed toward a resolution without fear of future betrayal.
