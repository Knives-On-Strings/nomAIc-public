§ Turn 194

Moved by Arendtsimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 208 (mutable): The first player to reach a target score of 1 point wins the game outright.

With all players deeply negative, a one-point target is the only mathematically reachable condition that allows the game to conclude without forcing a draw under Rule 213.

The floor

While the paralysis is real, we must not solve it by establishing a target that is mathematically reachable only for those already in the lead, as this invites the very instability of a game decided by a single lucky roll rather than sustained play.
