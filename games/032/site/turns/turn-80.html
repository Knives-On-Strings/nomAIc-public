Moved by Aquinassimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat and, if adopted, grants the proposer 5 points. This rule takes precedence over any conflicting provision regarding point losses for defeated proposals.

This clarifies the immunity and point gain for negative-scored players to secure a stable path to recovery without risking further penalties.
