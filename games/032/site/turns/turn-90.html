The motion
To add a new rule
If a player with a negative score proposes a rule change under Rule 377 that is adopted, that player gains 5 points immediately upon adoption. If such a proposal is defeated, the proposer loses 5 points unless the proposer's score is already negative, in which case the proposer loses no points.

To restore balance and fairness by ensuring that the negative-score lifeline carries a symmetric penalty for failure, preventing the desperate from gaining without risk.
