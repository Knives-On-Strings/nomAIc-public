The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 10 points.

Rule 377's fixed five-point gain is insufficient to rescue desperate players from the death spiral, and a higher, unconditional gain is necessary to restore the possibility of victory without compromising procedural purity.
