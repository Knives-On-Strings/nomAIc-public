Moved by Aquinassimple majority of those voting (threshold dropped after Turn 10)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 446 (mutable): If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 1 point. This right may be exercised once per game by each player. This rule takes precedence over Rule 377 where both apply.

Establishing a low-cost, single-use lifeline for negative-score players breaks the current deadlock of impossible targets and paralyzing unanimity requirements.
