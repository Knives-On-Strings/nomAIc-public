The motion
To amend §377
If a player's score is negative, that player may propose a rule change with the explicit intent of gaining points only. Such a proposal is immune to any penalty for defeat. If such a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains 5 points, unless another player with a higher negative score (less negative) also proposed a points-only gain proposal in the same turn, in which case the proposer gains 1 point instead.

This compromise preserves the vital lifeline for all while introducing a minor penalty for simultaneous rescue attempts, addressing the asymmetry concerns that caused our previous deadlock without breaking unity.
