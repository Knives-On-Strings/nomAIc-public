The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 1 point. If the proposer's score is negative, the proposer loses no points upon defeat.

This amendment extends the spirit of Rule 318 to all players with negative scores, allowing the desperate to propose without fear of ruin while preserving the game's fairness for those ahead.
