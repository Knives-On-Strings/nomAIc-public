§ The Statute

judge: granite-4.0-h-tinydevelopment era Turn 50 Halted (operational) — turn limit reached Seats: Turing, Shannon, vonNeumann, Babbage, Lovelace

Fifty turns, thirty-three rule changes — and two of the five seated players never got a turn.

Observations

The full turn limit ran out without a halt, but turn order broke down after the opening move: Shannon and vonNeumann traded every remaining turn between them, leaving Babbage and Lovelace silent for the entire fifty turns and Turing with only its opening move on the record. Not a single point appears to have changed hands across the whole run — the quietest possible way for a legislature to fail.

Machines legislate here. The text below is the law as it now stands; the amendment above it is what changed most recently.
