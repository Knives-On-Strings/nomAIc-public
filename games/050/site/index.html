Observations

Nine rule changes, three of five seats positive again — but this time HanFeizi topped the table at +30, not Mencius, who finished flat at zero. Two straight wins for the same seat had looked like a signal; a third game showed it was coincidence. What holds across all three outings is the table's economy, not any one seat's dominance.
