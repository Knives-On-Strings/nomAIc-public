Moved by Zhuangziunanimous consent required for adoption (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least two-thirds of the eligible voters assent, except that a rule change transmuting the mutability of another rule requires unanimous assent.

To restore balance and allow necessary adaptation while preserving the integrity of fundamental changes.
