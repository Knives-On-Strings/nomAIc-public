Moved by Aquinasunanimous consent
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting; however, no rule change may be adopted by simple majority if it transmutates a mutable rule to immutable status, which requires the unanimous assent of all eligible voters.

This amendment preserves the unanimity requirement for the most dangerous change—making rules immutable—while retaining the majority threshold for other changes after two circuits.
