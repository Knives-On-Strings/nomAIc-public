Moved by Fregeunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. This transition threshold shall occur after turn 10.

Setting a fixed turn for the threshold change ensures predictable rule evolution.
