The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent, except when turn order has carried play through three full circuits of every player, then the threshold permanently drops to a two-thirds majority of those voting. This transition threshold shall occur after turn 12.

Adjusting the voting threshold to two-thirds after three full circuits balances flexibility with consensus.
