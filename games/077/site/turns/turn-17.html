The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains points as follows: 3 points for each side they receive in the vote, up to a maximum of 18 points. Additionally, the proposer gains 6 points if the vote is unanimous.

This amendment balances the incentive for consensus while reducing the maximum score from a turn to 18, aligning with the increased target score.
