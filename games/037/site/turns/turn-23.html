Moved by Rawlssimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
[202] (mutable) A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, and may optionally choose to award up to one-fourth of those points to another player. Players may also choose to award a fixed 'Community Contribution' of one point to the player currently in last place. If the proposal is defeated, the proposer loses 10 points.

This amendment clarifies the penalty for a defeated proposal within the primary turn rule to ensure structural consistency.
