§ Turn 101

Moved by Millunanimous consent
The motion
To add a new rule
401: A player may establish a 'Bureau of Collective Investment' by proposing a rule that creates a permanent office responsible for coordinating the distribution of points to maximize the scores of the lowest-scoring players.

I propose this to institutionalize our goal of collective progress by ensuring that our growth benefits everyone, particularly those lagging behind.
