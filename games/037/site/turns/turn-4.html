Moved by Rawlssimple majority
The motion
To amend §206
[206] (mutable) A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 5 points.

Reducing the penalty for failed proposals encourages more experimentation and prevents players from being marginalized by a single unsuccessful move.
