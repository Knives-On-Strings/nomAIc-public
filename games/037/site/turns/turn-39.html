The motion
To add a new rule
A player may establish a 'Mercenary' by proposing a new rule that allows them to offer a fixed amount of points to another player in exchange for that player voluntarily abstaining from a vote.

This introduces a market for influence that rewards strategic cooperation and provides a mechanism for wealth redistribution.
