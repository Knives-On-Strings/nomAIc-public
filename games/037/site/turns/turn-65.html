The motion
To add §365
365: A player may establish a 'Ministry of Equilibrium' by proposing a rule that creates a permanent office with a specific set of duties and an elected official, provided the rule also establishes a system for the periodic rotation of that office among all players and specifies that the Ministry's primary function is the implementation of the Treasury's redistribution formula to maintain a balanced distribution of scores across the table.

To complete our institutional framework, we require an executive body to actively administer the redistribution of points to ensure social stability.
