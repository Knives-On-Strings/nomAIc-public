The motion
To add §482
482: A player may establish a 'Bureau of Institutional Equilibrium' by proposing a rule that creates a permanent office for the purpose of monitoring and balancing the various functions of the existing bureaus.

This final bureau provides the necessary oversight to ensure our complex administrative architecture remains balanced and serves the common good.
