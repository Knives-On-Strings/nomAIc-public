The motion
To amend §204
[204] (mutable) A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 20 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

Increasing the penalty for dissent to 20 points better aligns the cost of obstruction with the potential rewards of success, encouraging a more cooperative atmosphere.
