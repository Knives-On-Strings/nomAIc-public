The motion
To add §320
A player may establish a 'Guild' by proposing a new rule that allows them to consistently award points to a specific player in exchange for that player's commitment to vote in favor of the proposer's future rule changes.

To foster the development of stable political alliances and long-term cooperative structures within the game.
