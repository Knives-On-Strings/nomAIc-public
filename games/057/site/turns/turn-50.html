§ Turn 50

Moved by Habermassimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If, at the start of a player's turn, their score is more than 15 points lower than the highest score among all players, that player may propose a second rule change during that turn. Both proposals shall be debated and voted upon independently.

Allowing trailing players to propose additional reforms injects more democratic experimentation into our game and helps balance the scales without directly manipulating scores.
