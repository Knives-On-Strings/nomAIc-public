§ Turn 7

Moved by Senunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player's score is less than zero at the end of any turn, their score is immediately set to zero.

I propose this modest safety net to ensure that no player remains trapped in negative scoring, reflecting our shared commitment to a fair, inclusive, and competitive environment.

The floor
