The motion
To add a new rule
Whenever a rule change is adopted, every player whose score is strictly less than half of the highest score among all players immediately gains 2 points.

Building on our recent structural equity measures, this ensures that any player who falls significantly behind the leader receives a steady boost to keep the game competitive for everyone.
