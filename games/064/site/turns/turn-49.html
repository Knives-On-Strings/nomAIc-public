Moved by Millsimple majority
The motion
To amend §348
348. A player may contribute points to the shared pool under rule 338 to receive a 'Public Good' badge, which grants a +1 bonus to all future die rolls for the remainder of the game, provided the contribution is at least 5 points and the move is approved by a simple majority of eligible voters.

I propose adding a simple majority requirement to ensure that the 'Public Good' badge is a democratically sanctioned benefit.
