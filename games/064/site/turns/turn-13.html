§ Turn 13

Moved by Rawlssimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains a number of points equal to the value specified in rule 306, or 3 points if rule 306 does not exist. However, if the die roll is a 1, the player gains 3 points and the die is rolled again until a result of 2 or greater is achieved.

This change prevents the '1' result from being a dead end by allowing a re-roll, ensuring the game maintains its intended pace and reward structure.

The floor
