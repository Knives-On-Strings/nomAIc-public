§ Turn 17

Moved by Millsimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
308. If a player's turn results in a die roll of 1, they may choose to gain 3 points or 5 points. The choice is made by the proposer.

This provides a minor but helpful consistency for low rolls, mirroring the spirit of our previous refinements to rule 311.

The floor

Perhaps we could compromise by making it a fixed 4 points, or I can work with the group to refine the wording to ensure it doesn't conflict with our existing goals for simplicity.
