Moved by deBeauvoirsimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
332. A player may spend 10 points from their personal score to automatically succeed on any die roll for an adopted rule change, provided the change is approved by a simple majority.

This provides a secondary strategic option for risk mitigation that balances the existing 15-point cost of rule 331.
