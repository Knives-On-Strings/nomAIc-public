§ Turn 7

Moved by Millsimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposal was an amendment to rule 204, in which case the proposer gains 2 points.

This preserves the established 2-point consensus from the previous turns while maintaining the standard die roll for all other proposals.
