The motion
To add a new rule
Rule [X] (mutable): A player who has voted aye on a proposal may propose an amendment to that same proposal during the debate period immediately following its introduction, provided the amendment is accepted by the proposer before the vote concludes.

Allowing mid-debate refinements by the original proposer prevents wasted turns and fosters consensus by letting players correct errors before voting.
