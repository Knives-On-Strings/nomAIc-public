The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, and no more than five players have skipped their turns in the last six turns to balance point gains.

Adjust the threshold to allow for more strategic turn-skipping while preventing excessive point accumulation.
