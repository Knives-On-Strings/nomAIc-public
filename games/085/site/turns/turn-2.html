Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, but with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last four turns.

This amendment encourages strategic turn skipping while ensuring that the game remains balanced and engaging for all players.
