§ Turn 20

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 3 points, provided that no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, no more than four players have skipped their turns in the last five turns, the current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 2 points, no more than one player has skipped their turn in the last two turns, the proposed rule has not been defeated in the last two turns, and the cumulative skipped turns by all players do not exceed 10 in the current game.

To balance the game further by limiting excessive turn skipping and encourage more active participation.
