Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last three turns. Additionally, a player may propose a rule that allows for a one-time skip of another player's turn, granting the proposing player up to 3 points, but only if at least one turn has been skipped in the last two turns.

This amendment narrows the window for skipped turns to ensure more active participation while still allowing strategic point gains.
