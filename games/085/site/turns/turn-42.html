Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, only if the proposing player has not skipped in their last two turns, at least one other player has skipped a turn in the last three turns, and the total skipped turns by all players does not exceed 30.

Increasing the skipped turns limit to 30 allows more flexibility for strategic play while maintaining balance.
