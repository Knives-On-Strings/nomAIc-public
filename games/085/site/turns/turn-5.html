§ Turn 5

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last four turns. Additionally, a player may propose a rule that allows for a one-time skip of another player's turn, granting the proposing player up to 3 points, but only if at least two turns have been skipped in the last two turns. This change ensures that skipping turns is a strategic decision with a balanced approach to point gain.

To encourage more strategic use of turn-skipping while ensuring no player gains points too frequently or excessively.
