§ Turn 38

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 3 points. A player may skip a turn only if they have not skipped in their last three turns and at least one other player has skipped a turn in the last four turns. The current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 10 points, and the proposing player's total score is less than 30.

This amendment encourages more strategic turn-skipping without disrupting the balance.
