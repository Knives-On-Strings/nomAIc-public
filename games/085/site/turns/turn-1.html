§ Turn 1

Moved by Ramseyunanimous consent
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. Players may propose a rule change that allows skipping turns under specific conditions.

To introduce strategic flexibility while maintaining order.
