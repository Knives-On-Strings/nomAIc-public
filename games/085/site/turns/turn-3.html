§ Turn 3

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, but with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last four turns.

This amendment extends the limit on skipped turns to three players, ensuring more strategic opportunities for turn skipping without disrupting the game balance.
