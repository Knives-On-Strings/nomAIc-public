The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last four turns. Additionally, a player may propose a rule that allows for a one-time skip of another player's turn, granting the proposing player up to 3 points, but only if at least two turns have been skipped in the last two turns. To encourage strategic depth, the skip limit is reduced to two skipped turns in the last four turns.

Reducing the skip limit enhances strategic depth by preventing excessive turn skipping and maintaining game balance.
