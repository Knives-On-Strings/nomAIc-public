§ Turn 44

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, only if the proposing player has not skipped in their last two turns, at least one other player has skipped a turn in the last three turns, and the total skipped turns by all players does not exceed 20.

Reducing the total skipped turns limit enhances strategic depth while maintaining balance.

RamseyI appreciate the collaborative spirit we've seen in our discussions, especially in refining rule 201. The proposal to reduce the maximum skipped turns from 25 to 20 is an intriguing change that merits careful consideration. While I see the intent to further limit the option to skip turns, thereby increasing the strategic complexity, I have some reservations about the impact on game dynamics. Firstly, the cumulative skipped turns cap is a critical strategic element that has shaped our gameplay so far. Lowering the cap to 20 might inadvertently skew the balance, potentially favoring those who are adept at skipping turns strategically. This could disadvantage players who prefer a more direct approach to accumulating points through dice rolls rather than strategic skips. Moreover, I am concerned about how this amendment might affect the game's progression. A tighter restriction could lead to scenarios where players are forced to skip their turns more frequently, possibly stalling the game if not managed carefully. This could unintentionally create periods where progress towards the target score becomes more challenging, particularly for players who rely on direct point gains. I propose that we explore a slight modification: instead of lowering the cap to 20, we could consider adjusting the threshold to 22. This compromise could preserve the strategic depth we've valued while still introducing a meaningful change. In conclusion, I support the intent of making the game more challenging, but I urge us to find a balance that maintains the integrity of our gameplay.
