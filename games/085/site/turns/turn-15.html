§ Turn 15

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than four players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, no more than six players have skipped their turns in the last six turns, and the current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 5 points. Additionally, a player can only skip their turn if no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last two turns.

This change encourages more dynamic play and balances the advantage of skipping turns by introducing a tighter control on the frequency of skipped turns.
