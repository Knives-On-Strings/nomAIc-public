Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 3 points. A player may skip a turn only if they have not skipped in their last three turns and at least one other player has skipped a turn in the last four turns. The current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 20 points, and the proposing player's total score is less than 40.

Increasing the score difference and score threshold broadens strategic opportunities while maintaining balance.
