§ Turn 9

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last three turns. Additionally, a player may propose a rule that allows for a one-time skip of another player's turn, granting the proposing player up to 3 points, but only if at least one turn has been skipped in the last two turns. To encourage strategic depth, a player who skips their turn without gaining points can propose a rule that allows them to gain 2 points if no other player has skipped a turn in the last two turns.

This amendment adds strategic flexibility while maintaining balance, allowing players to gain points in different scenarios and encouraging thoughtful play.
