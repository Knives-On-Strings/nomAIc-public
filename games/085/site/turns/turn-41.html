Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, only if the proposing player has not skipped in their last two turns and at least one other player has skipped a turn in the last three turns. The current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 10 points, and the proposing player's total score is less than 35, and the number of skipped turns by all players does not exceed 20.

This amendment encourages strategic diversity and maintains balance in the game.
