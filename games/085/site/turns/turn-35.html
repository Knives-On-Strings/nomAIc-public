§ Turn 35

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 3 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, no more than five players have skipped their turns in the last five turns, the current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 10 points, no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last two turns, the proposed rule has not been defeated in the last two turns, the cumulative skipped turns by all players do not exceed 35, the total number of skipped turns by the proposing player is less than 25, at least two players have skipped their turn in the last four turns, the proposing player is not the current turn's mover, the proposing player's average skipped turns in the last five turns do not exceed their total skipped turns, and the proposing player has not proposed another skip in the last turn.

Adjusting the cumulative skipped turn condition to 35 to promote strategic flexibility while maintaining balance.
