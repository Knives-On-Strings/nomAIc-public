Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, no more than five players have skipped their turns in the last five turns, and the current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 3 points. Additionally, a player can only skip their turn if no more than one player has skipped their turn in the last two turns.

This change balances the strategic depth with fairness, ensuring more frequent opportunities to gain points while maintaining competitive tension.
