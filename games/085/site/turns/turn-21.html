§ Turn 21

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, no more than five players have skipped their turns in the last six turns, the current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 3 points, no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last two turns, the proposed rule has not been defeated in the last two turns, and the cumulative skipped turns by all players do not exceed 15 in the current game.

Increasing the point gain and adjusting conditions to balance risk and reward, ensuring more strategic depth in skipped turn decisions.
