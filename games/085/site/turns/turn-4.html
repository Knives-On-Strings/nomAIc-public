§ Turn 4

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last four turns. Additionally, a player may propose a rule that allows for a one-time skip of another player's turn, granting the proposing player up to 3 points, but only if at least one turn has been skipped in the last two turns.

Balances strategic depth with fairness, limiting excessive point gain while enabling tactical flexibility.
