Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, only if the proposing player has not skipped in their last two turns, at least one other player has skipped a turn in the last three turns, and the total skipped turns by all players does not exceed 20. Additionally, if a player has proposed three consecutive changes without adopting one, they must skip their next turn.

Introduces a new condition to balance the potential for frequent skipped turns, ensuring strategic depth.
