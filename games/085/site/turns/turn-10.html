Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing one player to skip a turn for gaining up to 5 points, provided that no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last three turns. Additionally, a player may propose a rule that allows for a one-time skip of another player's turn, granting the proposing player up to 3 points, but only if at least two players have skipped a turn in the last two turns, to maintain strategic balance.

This amendment aims to ensure a more balanced point distribution and encourage strategic play.
