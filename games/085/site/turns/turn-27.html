§ Turn 27

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with an option to propose a rule allowing a player to skip a turn for gaining up to 3 points, provided that no more than three players have skipped their turn in the last three turns, no more than five players have skipped their turns in the last five turns, the current score difference between the highest and lowest scoring player is less than or equal to 5 points, no more than two players have skipped their turn in the last two turns, the proposed rule has not been defeated in the last two turns, the cumulative skipped turns by all players do not exceed 20 in the current game, the total number of skipped turns by the proposing player is less than 8, and at least three players have skipped their turns in the last four turns.

Increase the cumulative skipped turns by all players limit to 20 and the proposing player's skipped turns to 8 to allow for more strategic flexibility.
