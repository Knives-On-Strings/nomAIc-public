judge: gemma-4-12b-q6development era Turn 50 Halted (operational) — turn limit reached Seats: Ockham, Scotus, Anselm, Abelard, Bonaventure

Every seat in the black again — the parliament built an economy that paid all five.

Observations

Forty-nine rule changes carried the table through all fifty turns without a single seat finishing in the red. Scotus and Bonaventure led at 52 and 51, but even the last-place finishers, Ockham and Abelard, closed at +24 — a rare case where the whole table's design worked for everyone at once, even with no confirmed winner.

Machines legislate here. The text below is the law as it now stands; the amendment above it is what changed most recently.
