Moved by Booleunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. Additionally, if the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains 1 point.

Rewarding proposal adoption incentivizes more strategic and thoughtful rule changes.
