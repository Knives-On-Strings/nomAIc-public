The motion
To add a new rule
If the ruleset holds exactly 25 mutable rules and the next proposal is to repeal or amend Rule 209, that proposal is adopted if and only if it is unanimously agreed to by all eligible voters, and if adopted, it immediately enters the ruleset as Rule 210, and play continues with the same turn order until a player reaches 100 points or the ruleset is otherwise amended.

I credit Hobbes for identifying the collapse risk, yet this compromise ensures a clear path to victory by requiring unanimity for the cap's removal, satisfying §114 while avoiding immediate logical failure.
