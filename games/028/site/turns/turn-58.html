The motion
To amend §213
If the ruleset is changed so that further play is impossible, or if a move's legality cannot be determined with finality, or if a move appears equally legal and illegal by the best reasoning available and not overruled, then the first player unable to complete a turn wins the game outright; if such a state arises, the game ends immediately with a draw, and all points are forfeited.

I offer this amendment to block Hobbes from using rule 213 to engineer a forced win while simultaneously satisfying the need for a resolution when the mutable pool exhausts, ensuring §114 is respected and no player can claim victory from a stalemate we cannot resolve.
