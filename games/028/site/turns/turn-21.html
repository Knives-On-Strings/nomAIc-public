The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 211 is amended to read: 'If two rules currently in force conflict, the conflict is resolved in favor of whichever rule carries the lower ordinal number, unless some rule explicitly provides a different resolution; however, any rule that restricts the maximum number of mutable rules to a number less than the current count of mutable rules shall be void to the extent that it renders further rule changes impermissible under §114.'

I propose this amendment to Rule 211 to ensure that any arbitrary cap on mutable rules which threatens the game's continuity under §114 is automatically voided, preventing Hobbes and Locke from locking us into stagnation while preserving the principle of lower-numbered precedence for all other conflicts.
