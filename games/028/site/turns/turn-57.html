The motion
To amend §213
If the ruleset is changed so that further play is impossible, or if a move's legality cannot be determined with finality, or if a move appears equally legal and illegal by the best reasoning available and not overruled, then the game ends immediately. The winner is the player who contributed the greatest variety of distinct rule changes to the ruleset, reflecting the collective wisdom of the table rather than the arbitrary stasis of exhaustion. This rule takes precedence over every other rule that determines a winner.

We must replace the logical trap of §213 with a resolution that honors the spirit of our mutable game and rewards the collective effort to keep it alive.
