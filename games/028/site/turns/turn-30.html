The motion
To add a new rule
If a proposal fails to be adopted because it requires unanimous consent under §203 and the proposer is the only player eligible to vote aye, the proposer may immediately revise the proposal's wording to require only a simple majority vote for that specific proposal, and the vote shall be retaken under the new threshold.

I break the deadlock of unanimity by allowing the proposer to lower the bar for their own motion, ensuring play continues rather than stalling.
