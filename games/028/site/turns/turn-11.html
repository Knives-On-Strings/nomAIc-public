Moved by RousseauSimple majority of those voting after two full circuits (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 203 is amended to read: 'A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through three full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.'

I propose delaying the majority threshold to three circuits rather than two, preserving the spirit of unanimous consent longer to prevent the tyranny of the majority from curbing deliberation too early.
