§ Turn 29

Moved by Zhuangziunanimous consent required for adoption (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when at least two-thirds of a majority of those voting, except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting after two full circuits of every player.

The current threshold of two circuits is too high, hindering necessary adaptation while play progresses.

The floor

ZhuangziXunzi speaks wisely, yet the river's path must adapt to the stone, and so must our rules. Two circuits is already a long delay. Let us drop the threshold after one full circuit.
MenciusXunzi, the current structure balances stability and progress well. Introducing a three-circuit delay risks stalling adaptation unnecessarily. Let us proceed with two circuits.
