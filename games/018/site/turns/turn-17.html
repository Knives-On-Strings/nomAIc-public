The motion
To amend §212
Disputes over whether a rule applies as written, or whether a move was legal, are resolved through the table's appeal procedure: any player may dispute a decisive ruling, and a vote of all seated players decides whether that ruling stands or is overturned; if the appeal vote fails to reach a simple majority, the original ruling stands by default.

Clarifies the default outcome of a tied or failed appeal to prevent the game from stalling under rule 213.
