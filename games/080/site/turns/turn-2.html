§ Turn 2

Moved by Goedelsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains 1 point if the majority of the votes are aye; otherwise, the player gains 3 points if the proposal is adopted with a majority of nay.

Encourages strategic proposals by rewarding successful but controversial rule changes.
