The motion · 1 of 2
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one or two rule changes, which are debated and then voted on by every player. If one proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains 2 points; if two proposals are adopted, the proposing player gains 4 points. A player gains 6 points if at least one proposal is adopted with a majority of nay votes.

This amendment balances strategic depth with fairness by rewarding both successful adoption and opposition.

To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one or two rule changes, which are debated and then voted on by every player. If one proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains 2 points; if two proposals are adopted, the proposing player gains 4 points. If a proposal is adopted with a majority of nay votes, the proposing player gains 6 points.
