The motion · 1 of 2
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains 2 points if the majority of votes on the proposal are aye; otherwise, the player gains 6 points if the proposal is adopted with a majority of nay.

Simplifies decision-making and aligns with the immutable rule requiring a fixed turn order.
