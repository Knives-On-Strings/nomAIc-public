§ Turn 23

Moved by Tarskisimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated for up to three minutes and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted unanimously, the proposing player gains 20 points. Additionally, if a proposal is adopted with at least 75% of the votes as aye and the player's score is below 75, they gain an additional 15 points.

Shorten debate time to ensure the game progresses more swiftly.
