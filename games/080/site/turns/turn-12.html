§ Turn 12

Moved by Goedelsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted unanimously, the proposing player gains 4 points. Additionally, if a proposal is adopted with at least 75% of the votes as aye, the proposing player gains 3 points.

Adjusting the percentage required for additional points to encourage collaboration and strategic play.
