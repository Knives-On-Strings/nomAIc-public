Moved by Goedelsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated for up to three minutes and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted unanimously, the proposing player gains 20 points. Additionally, if a proposal is adopted with at least 75% of the votes as aye and the player's score is below 50, they gain an additional 15 points.

Encourages collaboration and strategic play by increasing incentives for unanimous and broad consensus decisions.
