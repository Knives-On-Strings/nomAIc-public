The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted unanimously, the proposing player gains 8 points. Additionally, if a proposal is adopted with at least 75% of the votes as aye, the proposing player gains 5 points.

This change incentivizes broader consensus, aligning with the game's collaborative spirit.
