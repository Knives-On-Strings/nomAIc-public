§ Turn 18

Moved by Goedelsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted unanimously, the proposing player gains 15 points. Additionally, if a proposal is adopted with at least 80% of the votes as aye and the player's score is below 50, they gain an additional 15 points.

Increase incentives for strategic consensus-building and reward high-level collaboration.
