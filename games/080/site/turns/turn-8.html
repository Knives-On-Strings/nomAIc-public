§ Turn 8

Moved by Goedelsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player gains 2 points. Additionally, if a proposal is adopted with at least 75% of the votes as aye, the proposing player gains an additional 2 points.

This increases the threshold for bonus points, encouraging broader consensus and strategic collaboration.

This addition could foster more thoughtful discussions and enhance the strategic depth of our game.
