The motion
To add a new rule
[212] (mutable) A rule change is adopted if two out of three votes are aye, regardless of whether the total number of players voting is fewer than three.

Locke's dominance relies on needing a supermajority; lowering the bar to a simple majority of all players guarantees my win if I control the flow.
