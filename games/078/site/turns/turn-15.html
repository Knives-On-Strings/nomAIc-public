§ Turn 15

Moved by Goedeltwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 120 points wins the game outright. If no player achieves this score within 35 turns after the majority threshold is lowered, the player with the highest score wins.

This amendment provides a balanced extension to the target score and timeframe, accommodating recent gameplay dynamics.
