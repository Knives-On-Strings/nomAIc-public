To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted with a two-thirds majority, and a player who voted against an adopted change gains 5 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it. Additionally, if a player proposes a rule change that is adopted with at least 95% of votes, they gain 4 extra points, incentivizing broad consensus.

This encourages proposals that achieve near-unanimous agreement, fostering cooperative play.

I propose a compromise: a rule change adopted with at least 90% of votes should grant 4 extra points, while a unanimous vote grants 5 extra points. This maintains the incentive for consensus while recognizing the difficulty of achieving near-unanimity. Let's discuss and refine this balance.
