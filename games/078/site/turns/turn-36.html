§ Turn 36

Moved by Tarskisimple majority after two full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when at least 60% of the eligible voters vote aye, until every player has completed five full circuits of turns. Thereafter, the threshold drops permanently to a simple majority.

This change accelerates game progression, increasing strategic opportunities and maintaining fairness.

However, I am concerned that lowering the threshold to 60% prematurely could destabilize the game by enabling frequent and potentially disruptive rule changes early on. This could lead to a more volatile playing environment, where the focus shifts from strategic gameplay to constant rule manipulation. To balance the need for flexibility with stability, I propose a compromise: let's set the threshold at 70% for the first four circuits, then lower it to 60% for circuits five to seven, before finally adopting a simple majority thereafter. This gradual adjustment allows players to explore new strategies while maintaining a degree of stability in the early stages of the game. I am open to further discussion or negotiation to refine this proposal in a way that aligns with our collective goals.
