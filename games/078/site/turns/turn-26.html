The motion · 1 of 2
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The player may propose a second, non-competing rule change during their turn, provided it does not directly affect the current player's score or turn order. Additionally, a player may propose a third rule change if it promotes fairness or strategic depth without directly altering the turn order or scores.

This amendment introduces an additional rule change option to enhance strategic depth while maintaining fairness.
