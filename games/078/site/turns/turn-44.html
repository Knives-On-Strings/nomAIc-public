§ Turn 44

Moved by Goedelsimple majority after five full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that introduces a point-sharing mechanism, allowing players to mutually agree to distribute a pool of points among themselves, provided it passes a unanimous vote among all players and does not directly or indirectly benefit the proposer.

This encourages collaboration and strategic alliances, enhancing the game's dynamic.
