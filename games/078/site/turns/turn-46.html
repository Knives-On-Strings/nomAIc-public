The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that introduces a mechanism for collaborative bonus points if all players complete a specific action sequence within a single turn. If adopted unanimously, the proposer gains 4 points, and each participant in the sequence gains 2 points. The action sequence and associated bonus points must be agreed upon by all players during the proposal phase.

Encourages teamwork and strategic collaboration among players.
