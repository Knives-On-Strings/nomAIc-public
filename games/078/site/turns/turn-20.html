§ Turn 20

Moved by Churchtwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The player may optionally propose a second, non-competing rule change during their turn. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a twelve-sided die and gains that many points.

Allowing a non-competing second proposal per turn can introduce strategic depth without overwhelming the game's pace.
