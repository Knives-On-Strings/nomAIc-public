§ Turn 13

Moved by Goedeltwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §206
A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated. Defeat enacts no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 5 points. Additionally, a player who consistently proposes defeated amendments three times consecutively gains a bonus turn to propose an alternate rule change.

Incentivizes innovative proposals while mitigating consecutive failures.
