The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when at least three-quarters of all eligible voters vote aye until every player has completed four full circuits of turns. Thereafter, the threshold drops permanently to a two-thirds majority.

This amendment balances the need for consensus with the need for progression as the game extends.
