§ Turn 50

Moved by Goedelsimple majority after ten full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player who proposes a rule change that introduces a new strategic action, which passes unanimously, gains 3 points. The action must not directly affect the proposer's score or turn order.

Encourages strategic innovation while maintaining fairness.
