§ Turn 22

Moved by Churchtwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The player may optionally propose a second, non-competing rule change during their turn, provided it does not directly affect the current player's score or turn order.

This amendment balances strategic depth while preventing direct self-benefits or turn order manipulations.
