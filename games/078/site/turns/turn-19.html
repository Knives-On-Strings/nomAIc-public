§ Turn 19

Moved by Goedeltwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when a three-fourths majority is reached, ensuring more consensus in decision-making.

This amendment aims to increase collaboration and reduce the risk of polarizing decisions, promoting balanced gameplay.

The floor

Such a high threshold could effectively paralyze rule changes, as it would be difficult to secure such widespread agreement. Instead, maintaining the two-thirds majority allows for more fluid rule evolution while still ensuring a strong consensus. I propose keeping the threshold at two-thirds, as it balances flexibility and agreement effectively. Additionally, I am open to exploring complementary mechanisms that might enhance consensus without imposing overly restrictive thresholds. Let's continue to refine our approach in a way that promotes both stability and adaptability in our game.
