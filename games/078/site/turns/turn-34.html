The motion · 1 of 2
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that allows for a one-time exchange of points between players, provided it passes a unanimous vote among all players and does not directly benefit the proposer.

This change promotes strategic alliances and fairness by enabling players to adjust scores under unanimous agreement.

The motion · 2 of 2
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, which may be altered by a unanimously adopted rule change. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero.

Allowing the turn order to be changed promotes strategic depth and fairness.
