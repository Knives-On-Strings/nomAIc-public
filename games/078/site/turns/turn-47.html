§ Turn 47

Moved by Tarskisimple majority after five full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when at least 65% of the eligible voters vote aye, until every player has completed ten full circuits of turns. Thereafter, the threshold drops permanently to a simple majority.

Encourages more collaborative decision-making early in the game while allowing smoother progression later.
