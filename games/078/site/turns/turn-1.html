§ Turn 1

Moved by Tarskiunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through three full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a two-thirds majority of those voting.

This change balances strategic rule alterations with a need for broader consensus.
