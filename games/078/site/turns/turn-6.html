§ Turn 6

Moved by Goedeltwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 100 points wins the game outright. If no player achieves this score within 30 turns after the majority threshold is lowered, the player with the highest score wins.

This amendment ensures a clear path to victory while maintaining competitive tension.

This keeps the rule flexible and responsive to our gameplay dynamics.
