The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that introduces a collaborative challenge with a rotating proposer, requiring unanimous adoption, and completion within two turns grants a 4-point bonus to each participant. This rule must be adopted by a unanimous vote to be eligible.

Encourages strategic collaboration and fair point distribution.
