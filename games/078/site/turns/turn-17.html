The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. Additionally, the player proposing a rule change may declare a 'reflection period' of up to three minutes after debate closure, during which players may consider the proposal but cannot further debate or revise it. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a twelve-sided die and gains that many points.

This change facilitates thoughtful consideration before voting, enhancing strategic depth without extending debate time.
