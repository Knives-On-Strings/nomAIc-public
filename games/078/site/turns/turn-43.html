Moved by Tarskisimple majority after five full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To amend §344
A player may propose a rule change that allows for a one-time exchange of points between players, provided it passes a unanimous vote among all players and does not directly or indirectly benefit the proposer.

To enhance strategic depth and collaboration by facilitating point exchanges without direct gain to the proposer.
