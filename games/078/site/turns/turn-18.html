§ Turn 18

Moved by Tarskitwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A proposal to repeal a mutable rule must first be debated for a minimum of three minutes before voting begins.

Ensures thorough consideration of rule repeals to maintain stability.

The floor
