The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that allows for an additional turn under certain conditions. The conditions for granting an additional turn are: (1) the player must have successfully proposed and adopted three rule changes in a row, and (2) the player must not have forfeited the game. The player must declare their intention to use this additional turn at the beginning of their turn. Once used, the additional turn cannot be used again until the player successfully proposes and adopts three more rule changes in a row.

This proposal provides a balanced incentive for strategic rule changes while maintaining fairness.
