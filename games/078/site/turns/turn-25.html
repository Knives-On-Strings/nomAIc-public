Rule 206 states that a player who proposes a defeated amendment loses 5 points.

The motion · 2 of 2
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted with a two-thirds majority, and a player who voted against an adopted change gains 5 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it. Additionally, if a player proposes two successful rule changes in succession, they gain an additional 3 points.

Encourages strategic proposal of beneficial rule changes.

The floor

However, I propose an amendment: "Additionally, if a player proposes two successful rule changes in succession, they gain an additional 3 points. This bonus is only applicable if the player votes for both changes." This ensures that the proposer is genuinely invested in the changes they advocate.
GoedelI appreciate Tarski's suggestion to ensure proposers are invested in the changes they advocate. To build on that and encourage broader participation, I propose adding: "Moreover, if any player contributes a successful amendment to a rule change within the same turn, they receive 1 additional point."
