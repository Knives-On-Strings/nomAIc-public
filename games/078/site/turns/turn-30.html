To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted with a two-thirds majority, and a player who voted against an adopted change gains 5 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it. Additionally, if a player proposes a rule change that is adopted unanimously, they gain 3 extra points, and if it is adopted with at least 90% of votes, they gain 2 additional points.

Encourages more strategic voting by rewarding near-unanimous support.

To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. Additionally, a player may propose a second rule change during their turn, provided it promotes fairness or strategic depth without directly altering the current player's score or turn order. If the second change is adopted unanimously, the proposer gains a 2-point bonus.

Encourages strategic depth while maintaining fairness.
