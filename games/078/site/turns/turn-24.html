§ Turn 24

Moved by Churchtwo-thirds majority (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player who proposes a rule change that is adopted without opposition gains 3 points. This bonus does not apply if the player voted against the change.

Encourages collaborative rule-making by rewarding consensus.

The floor
Discussion · round 1
TarskiI appreciate Church's proposal for adding a point bonus for consensus-driven rule changes, as it encourages collaboration and strategic harmony. However, to ensure it does not unduly incentivize only the proposer, I propose an amendment: "A player who proposes a rule change that is adopted without opposition gains 1 point, and all players who voted for it also gain 1 point each." This amendment promotes broader cooperation among players while still rewarding successful consensus-building.
GoedelI appreciate Church's initiative to foster collaboration through this proposal, and I support Tarski's amendment to distribute points among all supporters. To further balance the game, I suggest adding a clause: "If a player who gains points from this rule change has already gained points from another rule change in the same turn, they only receive 1 point in total." This ensures a fair distribution and avoids excessive point accumulation by any single player.
ChurchI appreciate both Tarski's and Goedel's thoughtful amendments, which align well with the spirit of collaboration. To further ensure fairness, I propose an additional clause: "If a rule change is adopted without opposition, no points are awarded if the proposer has already proposed a successful change in their current turn." This will maintain competitive balance and encourage strategic planning among players. I am open to negotiating further amendments to refine this proposal.
KleeneI appreciate the collaborative spirit and thoughtful amendments by Tarski and Goedel. However, to ensure fairness, I propose an additional clause: "The point bonus applies only if the rule change does not directly impact the proposer's score or turn order." This ensures that the rule change remains neutral and encourages genuine consensus-building without self-serving motives. I am open to further negotiations.
PostI appreciate the collaborative effort and thoughtful amendments made so far. To further refine and balance the proposal, I suggest adding a clause: "The point bonus is only granted if the rule change is debated for at least two minutes, ensuring thorough consideration and strategic engagement." This encourages meaningful discussions and prevents hastily adopted changes. I am open to further negotiations to ensure this rule benefits all players fairly.
