§ Turn 48

Moved by Goedelsimple majority after ten full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A rule change that introduces a mechanism for periodic rule reviews, scheduled every ten turns, must be proposed and can be adopted with a simple majority. This review allows players to assess the current ruleset's impact on gameplay and propose amendments for better balance and fairness.

Facilitates ongoing adjustment and balance, ensuring the ruleset evolves with gameplay dynamics.

Let's negotiate on the frequency to perhaps every fifteen turns instead, allowing for thoughtful reflection and fewer interruptions.

However, I'm concerned about the potential for these reviews to be used strategically rather than for genuine balance and fairness. Let's work together to refine this proposal for the betterment of our game.
