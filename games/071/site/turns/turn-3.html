Moved by MarcusAureliussimple majority
The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 10 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it, unless the change specifically provides for a different point distribution.

This preserves the integrity of the majority's will while allowing for specific, balanced adjustments to point distribution.
