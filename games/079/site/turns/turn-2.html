The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when at least three eligible voters vote aye — reduced majority consent — except that once turn order has carried play through four full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

This change allows more flexibility in adopting rule changes by reducing the unanimity requirement, fostering collaborative progress in the game.
