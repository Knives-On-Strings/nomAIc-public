Moved by Churchsupermajority of four out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player may gain points from the change in their next turn.

This amendment allows proposers to benefit from their successful proposals, incentivizing strategic rule changes.
