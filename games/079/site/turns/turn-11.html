Moved by Tarskisimple majority of three out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §205
An adopted rule change takes effect immediately after the vote, but its benefits cannot be applied by the proposer in their next turn. A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated, enacting no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 10 points.

To ensure that rule changes cannot be exploited immediately for personal gain.
