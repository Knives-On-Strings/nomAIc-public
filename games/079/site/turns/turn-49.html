Moved by Goedelsimple majority of three out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §211
Disputes over whether a rule applies as written, or whether a move was legal, are resolved through the table's appeal procedure: any player may dispute a decisive ruling, and a vote of all seated players decides whether that ruling stands or is overturned. This rule takes precedence over every other rule that determines a winner, except rule 112.

This amendment prioritizes fair resolution while respecting the winning condition.
