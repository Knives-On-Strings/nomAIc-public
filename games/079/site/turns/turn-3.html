Moved by Churchsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §206
An adopted rule change takes effect immediately after the vote, allowing for the proposer to act on the change in their next turn if it benefits their strategy. A proposal that fails to be adopted is recorded as defeated, enacting no rule change: the ruleset is left exactly as it stood before the vote, the proposer's turn ends, and the proposer loses 10 points.

Encourages strategic thinking by allowing proposers to immediately leverage successful changes.
