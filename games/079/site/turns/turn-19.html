§ Turn 19

Moved by Tarskisupermajority of four out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws two six-sided dice and gains the sum of the two dice in points. A proposal may only be voted on once per player's turn.

To prevent repetitive voting, ensuring each turn is productive.
