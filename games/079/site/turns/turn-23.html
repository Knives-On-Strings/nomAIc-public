The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player may gain points from the change in their next turn. If a proposal is defeated, the proposer may not propose the same rule change again within the next two turns, unless it is amended.

This amendment balances strategic opportunities and fairness by allowing repeated proposals with amendments.
