Moved by Goedelsimple majority of three out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player may gain points from the change in their next turn. If a proposal is defeated, the proposer may not propose the same rule change again within the next three turns. Additionally, a player may gain up to 5 points for a proposal that fails due to lack of support if they can demonstrate a significant attempt to build consensus.

Encourages collaboration while maintaining strategic depth.
