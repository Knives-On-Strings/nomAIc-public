Moved by Tarskiunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted when a majority of eligible voters vote aye, except that the threshold for adopting a rule change remains at unanimous consent until a player achieves exactly 25 points.

This amendment encourages rule changes while ensuring a high threshold early in the game to maintain stability.

The floor
Discussion · round 1
TarskiI appreciate the effort to make rule changes more attainable by lowering the voting threshold, but I must oppose this proposal. Altering the threshold based on a specific point threshold could introduce instability by allowing players to reach that point cap and immediately change the ruleset, which undermines the durability of our institutions. Furthermore, this adjustment might unduly reward players who manage to hit the point cap, potentially disrupting the balance of the game. I propose instead that we consider a threshold change tied to the completion of a certain number of rounds, ensuring that all players have had a fair opportunity to participate before any rules are altered. This would preserve the integrity of the ruleset and ensure that changes are made with broad consensus. I am open to discussions on how we can adjust the rules to balance flexibility with stability, ensuring that our game remains fair and competitive for all players involved.
