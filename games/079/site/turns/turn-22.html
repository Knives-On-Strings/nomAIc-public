Moved by Goedelsupermajority of four out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when a simple majority of eligible voters, defined as three out of five, vote aye, with an additional condition that any rule affecting the voting threshold itself requires a unanimous vote.

This amendment maintains game flow while ensuring critical rule changes affecting voting require broader consensus.
