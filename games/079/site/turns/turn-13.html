§ Turn 13

Moved by Tarskisimple majority of three out of five (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws two six-sided dice and gains the sum of their faces as points.

This amendment aims to increase the potential points gain per turn, providing more strategic depth and motivation for rule proposals.
