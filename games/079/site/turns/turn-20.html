The motion
To amend §206
An adopted rule change takes effect immediately after the vote, but the proposer may gain points from the change in their next turn, and may not propose the same rule change again within the next three turns.

This ensures strategic diversity and prevents repetitive rule changes.

KleeneKleene: I appreciate Church's suggestion for allowing immediate re-proposal after a close defeat, as it balances fairness with strategic planning. This would encourage thoughtful proposals and give weight to broadly supported ideas, even if they don't pass on the first try.
