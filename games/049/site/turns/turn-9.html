§ Turn 9

Moved by Zhuangziunanimous consent required for adoption (§203, evaluated at turn 9)
The motion
To amend §203
Once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold for adoption becomes a simple majority of those voting, unless a rule explicitly provides otherwise, but a rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority by a vote of at least two-thirds of those voting.

To balance flexibility with stability, allowing majority adoption while retaining a strong consensus for fundamental changes.
