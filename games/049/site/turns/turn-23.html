§ Turn 23

Moved by Menciusunanimous consent to transmute (§109)
The motion
To transmute §201
and reclassify it between entrenched and mutable.

The fixed turn order, while stable, may limit strategic depth and adaptation. Allowing it to be mutable ensures the possibility of optimizing player order for fairness or engagement later, without compromising the current game's stability.
