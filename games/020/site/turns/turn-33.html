§ Turn 33

Moved by LockeTwo-thirds or more of eligible voters must vote aye
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player's score increases by ten (10) points. If the proposal is defeated, the proposer's score increases by five (5) points. This rule change may be adopted only with a simple majority vote.

Lowering the threshold in 203 from two-thirds to simple majority aligns the voting mechanic with rule 204, ensuring defeated proposals no longer stall play due to an unattainable supermajority requirement.
