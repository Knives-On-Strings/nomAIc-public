The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority, and the ruleset shall automatically shift to simple majority voting on the first proposal that achieves it.

This proposal ensures that once we break the unanimity deadlock, the system locks into a simple majority, making further progress possible for everyone.
