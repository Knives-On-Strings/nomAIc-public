Moved by LockeTwo-thirds or more of eligible voters must vote aye
The motion
To add a new rule
If a player proposes a repeal of rule 203, the repeal is adopted only if the proposal explicitly includes a replacement threshold of no lower than a simple majority.

I propose this to ensure that while we can lower the voting threshold, we cannot accidentally set it to zero or something that breaks the game, preserving the integrity of rule 214.
