Moved by Democritusunanimous consent (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points. If adopted, the proposing player also gains 10 points. If defeated, the proposing player loses 10 points.

This amendment balances risk and reward to encourage more strategic rule changes.
