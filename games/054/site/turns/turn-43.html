The motion
To add a new rule
If Rule 326 has been adopted and has not been repealed, the game ends immediately and the player with the lowest score wins, unless a player with a non-negative score exists, in which case that player wins. Rule 326 is hereby transmutable.

I propose a compromise that respects the victory already secured by Rule 326 while allowing us to transmute it, satisfying Rousseau's desire for mutability without the chaos of a failed vote.
