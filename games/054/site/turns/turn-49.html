The motion
To add a new rule
If the current voting threshold is simple majority, that threshold shall shift to unanimity for the specific purpose of adopting any rule that repeals Rule 326 or Rule 208.

I propose a temporary procedural pause to ensure that any move to alter our agreed victory condition requires full consensus, preventing hasty destabilization.
