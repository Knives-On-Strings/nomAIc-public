The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, except that once Rule 314 has been adopted, adoption requires only a simple majority of eligible voters.

This codifies the shift to simple majority triggered by Rule 314 while preserving the unanimous threshold for any changes made before that trigger, resolving the current stalemate.
