Moved by Rousseausimple majority (§203, §314)
The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 213 is amended to read: 'If the ruleset is changed so that further play is impossible, or if a move's legality cannot be determined with finality, or if a move appears equally legal and illegal by the best reasoning available and not overruled, then the player with the most negative score loses the game outright; all other players win jointly.' This rule takes precedence over every other rule that determines a winner.

Since we are trapped in negative debt with no path to zero, this amendment applies the precedence rule of 213 to determine a clear winner for the common good rather than indefinite stasis.
