§ Turn 36

Moved by Rousseausimple majority (§203, §314)
The motion
To add a new rule
The game ends immediately and the player with the highest score (least negative) is declared the winner.

This rule resolves the deadlock caused by negative scores and ensures the common good is served by ending play.
