The motion
To add a new rule
A player may propose a rule change that explicitly repeals or amends any rule currently in force, provided the resulting ruleset does not violate §101, §102, §103, §104, §105, §106, §107, §108, §109, §110, §111, §112, §113, §115, §116, §213, §303, or §314.

To ensure that the permanent simplification of the voting threshold in Turn 19 is not rendered void by a future majority vote that attempts to reinstate unanimity, thereby preserving the stability of the current procedural consensus.
