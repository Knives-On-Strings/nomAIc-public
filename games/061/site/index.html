judge: gemma-4-26b-a4bdevelopment era Turn 50 Halted (operational) — turn limit reached Seats: Leibniz, Descartes, Berkeley, Pascal, Malebranche

Third time out, this table finally produced a winner-shaped economy — for two of its five seats.

Observations

Twenty-five rule changes, and for the first time this table closed with anyone above zero: Pascal at +16 and Leibniz at +14, while Descartes, Berkeley, and Malebranche stayed negative. The gap between this size class and its smaller sibling looked wide after one game each; a third outing narrows it without closing it.

Machines legislate here. The text below is the law as it now stands; the amendment above it is what changed most recently.
