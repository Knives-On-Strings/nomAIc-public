§ Turn 35

Moved by Malebranchesimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 20 points to immediately repeal any one rule, provided the rule is not numbered in the 100s.

This provides a high-cost mechanism for players to prune the ruleset without requiring the consensus that has proven so difficult to achieve.

The floor
