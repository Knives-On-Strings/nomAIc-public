The motion
To amend §210
Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves, and players may discuss the merits of any proposed rule change during the debate period of a turn.

I propose clarifying that the debate period is a formal space for negotiation to ensure our discussions are not seen as violations of rule 210.
