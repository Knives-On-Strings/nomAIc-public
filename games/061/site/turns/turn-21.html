§ Turn 21

Moved by Leibnizsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero, and scores are capped at a minimum of zero.

Establishing a zero-floor for scores provides a more stable and predictable mathematical basis for the game's progression.
