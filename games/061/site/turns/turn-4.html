Moved by Pascalunanimous consent required until two full circuits have passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

Reducing the circuit requirement to one provides a necessary mechanism for rule evolution without sacrificing the integrity of the initial foundation.
