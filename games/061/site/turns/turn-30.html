§ Turn 30

Moved by Malebranchesimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 10 points to immediately repeal any one mutable rule, provided the repeal is proposed during their turn and adopted by a simple majority.

This introduces a strategic currency for institutional reform, allowing players to prune obsolete or harmful rules at a meaningful cost.
