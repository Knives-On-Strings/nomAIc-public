Moved by Descartessimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposing player chooses to forgo the roll, in which case they gain 0 points.

I propose to formalize the option to forgo a die roll, providing a way for players to manage their scores more precisely without needing a complex new rule.
