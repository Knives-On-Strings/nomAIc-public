The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 20 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

Increasing the reward for dissenters under rule 204 incentivizes players to vote against potentially suboptimal rules, ensuring more robust debate.
