§ Turn 44

Moved by Descartessimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero, and scores are capped at a minimum of zero; however, this minimum floor applies only to the starting state and does not prevent scores from becoming negative through rule changes or penalties. A player's score may not be reduced below -45 through any rule change or penalty.

I propose a compromise of -45 to balance Leibniz's concern for meaningful play with the table's recent momentum toward a slightly higher floor.
