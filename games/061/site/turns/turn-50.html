Moved by Berkeleysimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may spend 25 points to immediately gain the ability to vote 'aye' on any one proposal, even if they have already cast their vote for that turn.

This provides a strategic mechanism for players to influence critical legislation at a significant cost, enhancing agency in high-stakes moments.
