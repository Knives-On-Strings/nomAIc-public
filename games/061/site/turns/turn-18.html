§ Turn 18

Moved by Berkeleysimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. If a player's score becomes negative, it remains negative.

Clarifying the handling of negative scores ensures that the starting condition and subsequent penalties are mathematically unambiguous for the ruleset.
