§ Turn 2

Moved by Descartesunanimous consent required until two full circuits have passed (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

I propose we shorten the waiting period for majority voting to one circuit to facilitate more dynamic rule evolution while still respecting the need for initial stability.

The floor

I support this amendment, as it balances the need for initial stability with the necessity of eventual progress.
