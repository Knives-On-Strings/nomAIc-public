Moved by Pascalsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 20 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

Increasing the dissent reward to 20 points provides a sufficient procedural incentive for players to vote against potentially destabilizing changes even after the majority threshold is met.
