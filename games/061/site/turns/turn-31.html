Moved by Leibnizsimple majority after one full circuit of play (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through one full circuit of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting; however, once a simple majority threshold is reached, any player who votes against an adopted change gains 10 points and is counted as having voted 'aye' for the purpose of determining if a majority was reached.

This amendment consolidates the redundant scoring and voting mechanics of rules 203, 204, and 207 into a single, logically coherent provision.
