Moved by ZhuXisimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
When a player’s proposal is defeated, that player may, before the next player’s turn begins, publicly declare that they are taking a lesson from defeat. If they do, and if that player’s current score is below the median score of all players, they gain 1 point and may roll a die once. The die roll result is recorded publicly and has no immediate effect, but if that player’s next proposal is adopted, they gain additional points equal to the recorded die roll result, provided that proposal is adopted unanimously. A player may take a lesson from defeat at most once per full circuit of turn order.

This turns defeat into a learning opportunity for trailing players, rewarding persistence and eventual unanimous success.
