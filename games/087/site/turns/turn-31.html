§ Turn 31

Moved by Laozisimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
Whenever a player’s proposal is adopted, if that player’s current score is below the median score among all players at that moment, the proposer gains an additional point for each other player whose current score is higher than the proposer’s score, up to a maximum of 4 additional points.

This rule provides a modest, targeted boost to trailing proposers, scaled by their relative disadvantage, to encourage participation without excessive point swings.
