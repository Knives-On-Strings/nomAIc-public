The motion
To add a new rule
Before a player may propose a rule change, they must publicly declare their intended kind of change (add, amend, repeal, or transmute) and any target rule numbers. This declaration is binding; if the proposal as voted does not match the declaration, the proposal is considered illegal and is discarded without a vote. The proposer's turn ends immediately, and they lose 10 points.

This ensures proposals are transparent and deliberate, preventing last-minute ambush changes and preserving procedural integrity.
