The motion
To add a new rule
A player whose proposal is adopted may, as part of that same turn and before their point gain from that adoption is awarded, choose to forfeit those points and instead transmute one mutable rule to immutable. This transmutation requires no separate vote and does not count toward the 25‑mutable‑rule limit.

It introduces a strategic trade-off that strengthens the institutional framework without bypassing the voting threshold.
