The motion
To add §326
If the ruleset holds fewer than two mutable rules, a proposal to amend or transmute one of the remaining mutable rules is adopted upon a simple majority vote, regardless of the current voting threshold in Rule 203.

This creates a safe harbor to prevent Rule 114 violation when we are dangerously close to the two-mutable-rule limit.
