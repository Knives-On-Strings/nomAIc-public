The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, except that if a proposal has been debated for at least three turns without adoption, a simple majority shall suffice.

This compromise allows us to break the current unanimity deadlock while respecting Hobbes's caution by imposing a strict debate requirement first.
