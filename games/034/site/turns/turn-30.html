The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 203 is amended to require that a rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, unless the proposal itself explicitly states a lower threshold, in which case the stated threshold applies only to that specific proposal and any subsequent changes until the ruleset is amended to alter the default unanimity requirement again.

We must secure a mechanism to lower the unanimity threshold immediately without risking immediate voidance under Rule 114, by making the threshold change self-contained and reversible.
