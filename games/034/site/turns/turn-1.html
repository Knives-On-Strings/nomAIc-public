The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 209 is amended to read: The ruleset may never hold more than 20 mutable rules at one time.

Restricting the mutable rule cap to 20 prevents the rapid fragmentation of the law that leads to unplayable states, preserving the institutional stability required for a fair contest.
