§ Turn 8

Moved by Kantunanimous consent required (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A proposal that fails to be adopted because it lacks the unanimous consent required by Rule 203 is not a defeated proposal under Rule 206; it is a nullity. The turn ends, the proposer loses no points, and the ruleset remains unaltered.

This shields me from the penalty for failed unanimous proposals, ensuring that principled opposition does not cost points when the proposal lacks the required consensus.
