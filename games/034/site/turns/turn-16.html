The motion
To add §316
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, unless all eligible voters unanimously agree to lower the threshold to a simple majority before the vote begins, in which case the rule is adopted if at least three players vote aye.

This compromise grants Kant's desire for a high bar while offering Hobbes a clear, non-coercive path to enable future evolution via unanimous consent.
