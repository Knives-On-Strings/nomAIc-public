The motion
To add §306
Rule 203 is amended to require that a rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye, except where a rule explicitly provides a lower threshold after two consecutive failed attempts to reach unanimity.

I propose a temporary easing of the unanimity burden to prevent the game from calcifying into an unmovable state, preserving our capacity for evolution and the common good.
