The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 209 is repealed and replaced with: The ruleset may never hold more than 28 mutable rules at one time.

A ceiling of 28 preserves the necessary margin for repeal cascades while avoiding the arbitrary precision of 35 or the dangerous contraction of 22.
