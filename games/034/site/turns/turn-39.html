The motion
To add a new rule
If a rule change is adopted, and the resulting ruleset would render Rule 114 void by making it impossible for any future rule change to be proposed or adopted, then the proposed rule change is deemed adopted without triggering the void, and play continues with the new rules.

This surgical exception prevents the self-destruction of our legal system while preserving the ability to evolve.
