The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with a cyclical sequence ensuring each player has the opportunity to propose a rule change, allowing for one skipped turn every four cycles to expedite rule adoption.

Introduce a mechanism to potentially skip a turn every fourth cycle to speed up rule adoption.
