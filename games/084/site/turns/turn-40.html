The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, debating it, voting on it, and then rolling a twenty-sided die to gain points. If the roll is a '0', the player loses 5 points; if the roll is a '1', the player gains 1 bonus point. If a player rolls a '20', they gain 5 bonus points. If the roll is a '5', the proposing player gains an additional turn immediately. To encourage strategic depth, introduce a rule where if a player rolls a '15', they may choose to split their roll between two players of their choice, granting each half the points of the roll rounded down.

Enhancing strategic interaction and depth by allowing players to influence others' scores.
