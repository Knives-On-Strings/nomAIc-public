Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player, followed by a dice roll to gain points. If the proposing player's turn ends with a roll of zero, they lose 5 points.

This amendment ensures that players cannot gain points from rolling a zero, maintaining the competitive nature of the game.
