§ Turn 17

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when the votes of a majority of eligible voters are aye, except that once turn order has carried play through four full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

This amendment ensures a smoother transition to simpler voting thresholds after initial rounds of unanimous voting, balancing strategic depth with practicality.
