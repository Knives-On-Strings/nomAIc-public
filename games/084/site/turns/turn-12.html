The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 140 points wins the game outright, with each turn consisting of proposing, debating, and voting on a rule change, followed by a dice roll to gain points.

Adjusting the target score to 140 points balances competitiveness with playability, ensuring a longer and more strategic game.
