The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, debating it, voting on it, and then rolling a twenty-sided die to gain points. If the roll is a '0', the player loses 5 points; if the roll is a '1', the player gains 1 bonus point.

Increase the potential maximum gain from a turn to align with the target score of 150 points, thereby encouraging more strategic play.
