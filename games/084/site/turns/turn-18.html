§ Turn 18

Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player, followed by a dice roll to gain points. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a twelve-sided die and gains that many points, with a minimum of one point.

This amendment ensures the turn structure is clear and consistent, enhancing the strategic flow of the game.
