The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 120 points wins the game outright. Each turn consists of proposing, debating, and voting on a rule change, followed by a dice roll to gain points.

Adjusting the target score to 120 points ensures more dynamic gameplay and encourages strategic rule changes.
