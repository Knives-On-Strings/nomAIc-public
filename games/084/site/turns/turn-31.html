Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when the votes of a two-thirds majority of eligible voters are aye, except that once turn order has carried play through four full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a two-thirds majority of those voting.

Adjusting the voting threshold to two-thirds majority ensures more consensus while still allowing for strategic decisions.
