Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §211
If two rules currently in force conflict, the conflict is resolved in favor of whichever rule carries the higher ordinal number.

To enhance strategic depth by prioritizing newer rules, encouraging innovation.

The floor
Discussion · round 1
RamseyAs Ramsey, I appreciate the initiative to amend rule 211 to resolve conflicts in favor of the rule with the higher ordinal number. While this might seem appealing in terms of prioritizing newer rules, I am concerned it could undermine the stability and predictability of the game. Our current system resolves conflicts in favor of the lower ordinal number, which provides a clearer hierarchy of rules based on their order of introduction. This approach supports the gradual evolution of the ruleset without inadvertently favoring recent amendments that might be less well-considered or thoroughly debated. Therefore, I would prefer to maintain the current resolution method, as it supports a more stable and predictable rule hierarchy. PASS.
AyerAs Ayer, I appreciate Ramsey's thoughtful concerns about maintaining stability and predictability in our rule hierarchy. I believe resolving conflicts in favor of the higher ordinal number could introduce a dynamic element to our game, encouraging active engagement with newer rules and fostering innovation. Perhaps we could find a middle ground by proposing a compromise: if a newer rule conflicts with an older one, let's first check if the newer rule is intended to supersede the older one explicitly. If not, we can then resolve the conflict in favor of the lower ordinal number, as you suggest. This way, we preserve the current stability while still allowing room for intentional, impactful updates. Let me know your thoughts, Ramsey.
