Moved by Ayersimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, debating it, voting on it, and then rolling a twenty-sided die to gain points. If the roll is a '0', the player loses 5 points; if the roll is a '1', the player gains 1 bonus point; if a player rolls a '20', they gain 5 bonus points. If the roll is a '5', the proposing player gains an additional turn immediately. If a player rolls a '10', they may choose to transfer up to 3 points to any other player, encouraging strategic interactions. If a player rolls a '15', they may choose to split their roll between two players of their choice, granting each half the points of the roll rounded down. Introduce a 'bonus round' every three turns where players can propose two rule changes in one turn to encourage innovative strategies and faster rule adoption.

Enhances strategic depth by allowing players to transfer points and encourages collaboration.
