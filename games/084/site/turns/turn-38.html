The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, debating it, voting on it, and then rolling a twenty-sided die to gain points. If the roll is a '0', the player loses 5 points; if the roll is a '1', the player gains 1 bonus point. Additionally, if a player rolls a '20', they gain 5 bonus points to encourage more strategic play. If the roll is a '10', the proposing player gains an additional turn immediately, to encourage more dynamic gameplay.

Adding an immediate extra turn for rolling a '10' can create more dynamic opportunities for rule changes and strategic advantage.
