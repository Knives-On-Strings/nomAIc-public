§ Turn 4

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with a cyclical sequence ensuring each player has the opportunity to propose a rule change. No player's turn may be skipped.

Clarify the cyclical turn order to reinforce the structure and ensure no skipped turns.
