§ Turn 25

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with a cyclical sequence ensuring each player has the opportunity to propose a rule change, allowing for one skipped turn every three cycles to expedite rule adoption.

Reducing the frequency of skipped turns will balance the pace of rule adoption with fairness in turn distribution.
