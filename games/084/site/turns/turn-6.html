Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through four full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting.

Ensuring a balance between innovation and stability, I propose increasing the number of full cycles required before the voting threshold changes to a simple majority to four, ensuring more deliberation.
