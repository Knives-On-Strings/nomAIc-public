The motion
To amend §205
A rule change takes effect immediately upon the completion of its vote, with a grace period of one turn for contestation. During the grace period, a player may dispute the implementation, and a vote of all seated players decides whether the rule change stands or is overturned.

The amendment introduces a structured grace period to ensure careful consideration and contestation of rule changes.
