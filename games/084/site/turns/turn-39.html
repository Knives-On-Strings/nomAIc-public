The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, debating it, voting on it, and then rolling a twenty-sided die to gain points. If the roll is a '0', the player loses 5 points; if the roll is a '1', the player gains 1 bonus point. If a player rolls a '20', they gain 5 bonus points to encourage more strategic play. If the roll is a '5', the proposing player gains an additional turn immediately, encouraging dynamic gameplay.

Introducing a '5' roll for an additional turn adds dynamic potential to gameplay.
