§ Turn 27

Moved by Rylesimple majority
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins, with a cyclical sequence ensuring each player has the opportunity to propose a rule change, allowing for one skipped turn every three cycles to expedite rule adoption. Additionally, introduce a 'bonus round' every ten turns where players can propose two rule changes in one turn to encourage innovative strategies and faster rule adoption.

The bonus round will enhance strategic depth and player engagement by allowing more dynamic rule changes and potentially faster progression.
