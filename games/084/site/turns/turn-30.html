§ Turn 30

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. Following the vote, the player rolls a twelve-sided die to gain points. If the roll is a '0', the player loses 5 points; if the roll is a '1', the player gains 1 bonus point.

To enhance strategic depth and scoring opportunities within the game.
