§ Turn 19

Moved by Ramseysimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the proposing player suggesting exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player, followed by a dice roll to gain points. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a twenty-sided die and gains that many points, with a minimum of one point.

Adjusting the dice to a twenty-sided die increases potential points, incentivizing rule changes and enhancing strategic depth.
