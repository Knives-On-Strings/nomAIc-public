The motion
To amend §205
A rule change takes effect immediately upon the completion of its vote, unless a player invokes the grace period to contest its implementation. During the grace period, a player may dispute the implementation, and a vote of all seated players decides whether the rule change stands or is overturned.

Introducing a grace period allows for contesting rule implementations, ensuring fairness and strategic depth.
