§ Turn 2

Moved by Epictetussimple majority
The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 10 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it, unless the rule change specifically provides for a different reward for dissenting voters.

This allows for more nuanced rewards for dissent, which can be used to build consensus on difficult changes.
