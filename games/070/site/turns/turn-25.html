§ Turn 25

Moved by MarcusAureliussimple majority
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 100 points wins the game outright, unless the game is declared a stalemate by a majority of players due to an inability to reach the goal.

Establishing a clear path to resolve stalemates ensures the game remains playable and fair for all participants.
