Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer must submit the proposal at least three turns in advance and provide a rationale that is at least 20 words long, includes at least one concrete example or precedent, aligns with the current ruleset, and addresses potential impacts on future play. A player may gain 5 additional points for unanimous consent, 3 points for a simple majority, and 2 points if the rationale includes a historical precedent. A proposal must be reviewed by a committee, which must include at least one member from each quartile of the current scores, and must provide a brief explanation of their reasoning.

This change ensures thorough consideration of rule impacts, promoting strategic depth and fairness.
