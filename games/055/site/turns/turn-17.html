Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer must submit the proposal at least two turns in advance and provide a rationale that is at least 10 words long, includes at least one concrete example or precedent, and aligns with the current ruleset. The designated committee, consisting of at least one member from each quartile of the current scores, must review and provide a brief explanation of their reasoning. A player may gain 5 additional points for unanimous consent and 3 points for a simple majority. The rationale must be presented in a standardized format including bullet points to outline key points, ensuring clarity and transparency.

Extending the submission deadline encourages thoughtful proposals and aligns with our emphasis on transparency and clarity.
