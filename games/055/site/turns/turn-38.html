§ Turn 38

Moved by Fregesupermajority (3/5) until after three full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when a supermajority (3/5) of eligible voters vote aye until after each player has completed two full circuits of turns, at which point it requires a simple majority.

This amendment shortens the duration of the supermajority requirement to two circuits, aligning adoption conditions with faster strategic adaptations.
