§ Turn 36

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when a supermajority (3/5) of eligible voters vote aye until after each player has completed three full circuits of turns, at which point it requires a simple majority.

This amendment ensures more stable rule changes by requiring broader consensus initially, aligning with my value of durable institutions.
