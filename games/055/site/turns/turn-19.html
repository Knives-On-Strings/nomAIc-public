The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer must submit the proposal at least two turns in advance and provide a rationale that is at least 25 words long, includes at least two concrete examples or precedents, and aligns with the current ruleset. The designated committee, consisting of at least one member from each quartile of the current scores, must review and provide a brief explanation of their reasoning. A player may gain 5 additional points for unanimous consent, 3 points for a simple majority, and 2 points if the rationale includes a historical precedent.

This amendment increases proposal preparation time and rationale detail to enhance strategic planning and discussion quality.
