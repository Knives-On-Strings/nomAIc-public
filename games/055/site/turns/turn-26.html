Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer must submit the proposal at least three turns in advance and provide a rationale that is at least 25 words long, includes at least one concrete example or precedent, aligns with the current ruleset, and addresses potential impacts on future play. A proposal must be reviewed by a committee, which must include at least one member from each quartile of the current scores and at least one player with a score below the median, and must provide a brief explanation of their reasoning. To ensure transparency, the rationale must also include a section explicitly addressing how the proposal maintains strategic depth while minimizing undue advantage.

Extending rationale length to 25 words ensures more thoughtful proposals, maintaining strategic depth and fairness in rule changes.
