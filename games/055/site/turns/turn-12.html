§ Turn 12

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §201
Players take one fixed turn order, set before play begins. Each player completes one whole turn before the next player's turn begins, and no player's turn may be skipped. Every player's score starts at zero. Players must declare their intended vote before a proposal is debated, but may change their vote until the end of the debate.

Enhances transparency by clarifying the voting process.
