§ Turn 9

Moved by Quinesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer may gain 5 additional points if they secure unanimous consent and 3 additional points if they secure a simple majority, but must also propose a rationale that is at least 10 words long and includes at least one concrete example or precedent.

This change encourages more informative rationales, aiding in clearer decision-making.
