§ Turn 49

Moved by Fregesimple majority after two full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A rule change proposal must include a 'strategic implications' section, detailing potential long-term effects on gameplay. The reflection requirement is eliminated. Proposals must be submitted at least three turns before voting, allowing sufficient time for review.

This amendment simplifies the proposal process by removing the reflection requirement, which has been a point of contention, and extends the submission timeline to enhance deliberative review.
