Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer may gain 5 additional points if they secure unanimous consent and 3 additional points if they secure a simple majority, but must also propose a rationale that is at least 10 words long and includes at least one concrete example or precedent. Each proposal must be submitted at least one turn in advance for review by a designated committee, which must consist of at least one member from each quartile of the current scores.

Enhancing committee diversity ensures balanced perspectives in rule reviews.
