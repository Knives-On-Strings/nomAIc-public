§ Turn 6

Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer may gain 5 additional points if they secure unanimous consent and 2 additional points if they secure a simple majority.

This change balances reward for collaboration and individual achievement.

I propose a middle ground: increase the points for unanimous consent to 4 but retain the current 3 points for simple majority approval, maintaining a balance between reward and stability.
