§ Turn 16

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer must submit the proposal at least one turn in advance and provide a rationale that is at least 10 words long, includes at least one concrete example or precedent, and aligns with the current ruleset. The designated committee, consisting of at least one member from each quartile of the current scores, must review and provide a brief explanation of their reasoning. A player may gain 5 additional points for unanimous consent and 3 points for a simple majority. Every player's vote carries equal weight.

Ensures clarity and fairness while maintaining alignment with existing rules.
