Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer may gain 5 additional points if they secure unanimous consent and 3 additional points if they secure a simple majority, but must also propose a rationale that is at least 15 words long and includes at least two concrete examples or precedents. Each proposal must be submitted at least two turns in advance for review by a designated committee, which must consist of at least two members from each quartile of the current scores. To promote fairness, the committee's decision must include a brief explanation of their reasoning. Furthermore, each player must provide a brief summary of how the proposed rule aligns with the current ruleset.

Extending rationale requirements enhances proposal quality and strategic depth, benefiting all players.
