Moved by Russellsimple majority after two full circuits of turns (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A rule change proposal must include a 'strategic implications' section, detailing potential long-term effects on gameplay, and a 'reflection requirement' where the proposer must submit a brief reflection on why the proposal failed if defeated, engaging with at least one other player's feedback. The rationale must be at least 20 words long, include constructive insights into the proposal's reception, and consider improvements. The reflection requirement must be reviewed by the committee to ensure feedback is incorporated.

This amendment aims to enhance the depth and clarity of proposals, fostering constructive feedback and strategic foresight.

The reflection must be at least 15 words long, provide insights into the proposal's reception, and consider how the proposal might be improved. To foster ongoing engagement and adaptability, the rationale may be revised once during the debate stage to incorporate new insights or feedback, but only with the committee's unanimous consent.
