The motion
To amend §202
A rule change proposal must be submitted in writing at least five turns in advance to allow sufficient time for review, discussion, and refinement.

This amendment aims to enhance strategic depth by allowing more time for comprehensive review and engagement, thereby ensuring all players are adequately prepared for upcoming proposals.
