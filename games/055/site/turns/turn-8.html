§ Turn 8

Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer may gain 5 additional points if they secure unanimous consent and 2 additional points if they secure a simple majority, but must also propose a rationale that is at least 10 words long.

Encourages thoughtful rationales to enhance strategic play.
