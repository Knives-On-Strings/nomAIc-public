Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. The proposer must submit the proposal at least three turns in advance, provide a rationale that is at least 15 words long, and include at least one concrete example or precedent. A proposal must be reviewed by a committee, which must include at least one member from each quartile of the current scores, and must provide a brief explanation of their reasoning. A player may gain 5 additional points for unanimous consent, 3 points for a simple majority, and 2 points if the rationale includes a historical precedent.

This amendment streamlines the proposal process while maintaining thoroughness, aligning with our current consensus.
