As it now reads
§202
A rule change proposal must include a 'strategic implications' section and be submitted at least four turns before voting to allow sufficient time for review. Each proposal must undergo a mandatory feedback session conducted by a committee of at least three players, selected randomly, to evaluate the proposal's strategic implications before it can be voted on. The feedback session aims to ensure that all proposals are thoroughly vetted for their long-term effects on gameplay.
