The motion
To add a new rule
If a proposal is adopted, the proposer gains points equal to the die roll result, unless rule 314 is in effect, in which case the proposer gains the points specified in rule 314, provided no other rule explicitly overrides this mechanism, and provided that the adopted rule does not result in a net loss of points for the proposer across the entire table, unless rule 309 is in effect, in which case the proposer receives 5 points.

I must re-establish the foundational mechanism of reward to ensure procedural consistency.
