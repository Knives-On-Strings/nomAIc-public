Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 87 points wins the game outright; however, this target score is subject to a review vote after every full circuit of play, during which it may be adjusted by at most 5 points up or down.

Adjusting the target score to 87 points may encourage strategic rule changes to hasten the end of the game.
