§ Turn 12

Moved by Quinesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly two rule changes, which are debated and then voted on by every player in sequence. If both proposals are adopted, the proposing player throws a ten-sided die twice and gains that many points for each.

Encourages more strategic depth and interaction.

The floor
Discussion · round 1
FregeThe proposal to allow two rule changes per turn introduces unnecessary complexity and unpredictability, potentially destabilizing the game's strategic balance. Maintaining a single proposed change per turn ensures clarity and focused debate, aligning with my preference for durable institutions. I urge reconsideration of the proposal to keep the rule as is.
