The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal. Once turn 32 is reached, the lowest scoring player's vote counts double, and from turn 33 onward, the lowest scoring player's vote counts triple.

Ensures strategic depth and fairness by extending vote power incrementally.
