The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 85 points wins the game outright; however, this target score is subject to a review vote after every full circuit of play, during which it may be adjusted by at most 5 points up or down.

Lowering the target score gives more players a chance to win, promoting a more competitive and engaging game environment.
