§ Turn 13

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal, and every player's vote carries equal weight, except that during the last three turns, the vote of the player in first place counts double.

This adjustment ensures a strategic endgame by giving the leading player more influence.
