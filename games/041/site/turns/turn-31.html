§ Turn 31

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal. Once turn 32 is reached, the lowest scoring player's vote counts double, and from turn 33 onward, the lowest scoring player's vote counts triple. Once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority of those voting (rule 203), the lowest scoring player's vote counts quadruple from turn 32 onward.

This amendment ensures greater influence for the lowest scoring player when voting becomes more strategic.
