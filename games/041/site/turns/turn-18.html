Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 95 points wins the game outright; however, this target score is subject to a review vote after every full circuit of play, during which it may be adjusted by at most 5 points up or down. Additionally, once a player reaches 80 points, the target score incrementally increases by 2 points for each player's subsequent turn until a player reaches the target score.

This amendment balances the dynamic nature of the game by ensuring competitive play even as players approach the target score.
