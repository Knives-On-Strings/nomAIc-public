The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 85 points wins the game outright, subject to a review vote after every full circuit of play, during which it may be adjusted by at most 3 points up or down.

Adjusting the target score ensures strategic flexibility and engagement until the game's conclusion.
