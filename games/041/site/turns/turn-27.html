The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal. The lowest scoring player's vote counts triple once turn 32 is reached, and from turn 33 onward, it counts quadruple. The player's vote returns to normal from turn 35 onward.

Enhances strategic depth by further amplifying the influence of the lowest scoring player.
