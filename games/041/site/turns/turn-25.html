§ Turn 25

Moved by Fregesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal. However, once the game reaches turn 35 or later, the vote of the player with the lowest score counts double, and from turn 36 onward, the lowest score player's vote counts triple.

This change further amplifies the influence of lower-scored players, aligning with strategic balance.
