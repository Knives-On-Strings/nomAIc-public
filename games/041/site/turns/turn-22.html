Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §208
The first player to reach a target score of 88 points wins the game outright; however, this target score is subject to a review vote after every full circuit of play, during which it may be adjusted by at most 3 points up or down.

This adjustment aims to balance competitiveness and strategic depth, considering previous attempts and consensus-building efforts.
