Moved by Russellsimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal, and from turn 31 onward, the lowest scoring player's vote counts double. After turn 32, this effect resets.

This proposal balances strategic depth and fairness by allowing the lowest scoring player more influence mid-game without overwhelming the dynamics.
