§ Turn 19

Moved by Quinesimple majority (§203)
The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal, and every player's vote carries equal weight. However, once the game reaches turn 30 or later, the vote of the player with the lowest score counts double.

This amendment balances power dynamics by favoring players lagging behind.
