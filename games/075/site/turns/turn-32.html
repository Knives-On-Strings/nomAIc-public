Moved by ZhuXisimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
When a player’s turn begins, if that player’s score is negative, they may, before proposing a rule change, declare that they are invoking a hardship adjustment. Upon such a declaration, the player’s score is immediately set to zero, but the player may not gain points from any rule change proposed on that same turn.

This offers a one-time reset for deeply negative players to re-engage without trapping them in despair, while preventing immediate point harvesting.
