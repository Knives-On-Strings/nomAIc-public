§ Turn 15

Moved by ShaoYongsimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may, once per full circuit of turn order, voluntarily reduce their own score by exactly 10 points; if they do, they may immediately transfer those 10 points to any other player of their choice.

This adds a strategic tool for collaboration and point management, fostering alliances while maintaining transparency.
