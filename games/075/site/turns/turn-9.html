The motion
To add a new rule
A player who votes nay on a proposal that is adopted may, before the next proposal is voted on, formally request a ruling on whether the adopted change conflicts with an immutable rule. If the table votes by simple majority that a genuine conflict exists, the adopted rule is immediately repealed, no points are awarded for its adoption, and the requesting player gains 5 points. A player may make such a request at most once per full circuit of turn order.

This provides a procedural check against inadvertently violating immutable rules, rewarding vigilance while preserving game integrity.
