§ Turn 19

Moved by DongZhongshusimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may, at the start of their turn before proposing a rule change, publicly identify a contradiction between two existing mutable rules that has not previously been identified. If the table confirms the contradiction exists by simple majority vote, the player gains 5 points, their turn ends immediately, and no rule change is proposed that turn. A player may earn this reward at most once per full circuit of turn order.

This rewards vigilance in detecting rule conflicts before they cause problems, consistent with existing conflict-resolution mechanisms.
