The motion
To add a new rule
A player who, after a rule change is adopted, publicly identifies a previously unnoticed drafting error — defined as a clear violation of the author's expressed intent, not mere unintended consequences — and whose identification is confirmed by a vote of the table, gains 3 points. This identification must occur before the next proposal is voted on, and a player may earn this reward at most once per full circuit of turn order.

This corrects a persistent gap in the ruleset by rewarding diligent proofreading that preserves institutional integrity, aligning with the spirit of rule 308 but focusing narrowly on errors that subvert intent.
