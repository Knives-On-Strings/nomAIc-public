Moved by WangYangmingsimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To amend §308
A player who, after a proposal is adopted, publicly identifies a previously unnoticed contradiction between an immutable rule and any other rule currently in force, and whose identification is confirmed by a simple majority vote of all eligible voters (conducted before any further proposal is voted on), earns 3 points. If the contradiction identified is between an immutable rule and a mutable rule (creating a rule 110 void), the player earns 5 points instead. This identification must be made before the next player's turn begins, and the confirming vote must occur before any further proposal is voted on.

To clarify rule 308's coverage of rule 110 voids and establish a tiered reward that properly values foundational integrity.
