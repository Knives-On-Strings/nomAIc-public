§ Turn 37

Moved by ZhuXisimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To amend §328
A player who, during debate on a proposal, publicly identifies a previously unnoticed contradiction between the proposal and one or more immutable rules, and whose identification is logged before the vote, must, before the vote, either withdraw the proposal or amend it to remove the contradiction. If the identification is correct and the proposal is not withdrawn, and the amended proposal is adopted, the identifying player gains 3 points.

This amendment ensures rule 328's requirement is enforced before voting, aligning it with rule 304's structure while retaining its distinct reward for early vigilance.
