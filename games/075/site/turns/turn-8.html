Moved by WangYangmingsimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player who, after a proposal is adopted, publicly identifies a previously unnoticed contradiction between the newly adopted rule and any existing rule, and whose identification is confirmed by a vote of the table, gains 5 points. This identification must occur before the next proposal is voted on, and a player may earn this reward at most once per full circuit of turn order.

This encourages vigilance for coherence, rewarding players who spot contradictions early and helping keep the ruleset logically sound.
