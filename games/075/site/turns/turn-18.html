Moved by WangYangmingsimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player who, upon the adoption of a rule change, publicly identifies a genuine drafting error in the adopted text — an error that, if left uncorrected, would make the rule internally inconsistent, unenforceable, or in direct conflict with an immutable rule — may, before the next proposal is voted on, propose a purely corrective amendment to fix that error. If the corrective amendment is adopted by unanimous consent, the identifying player gains 5 points. A player may earn this reward at most once per full circuit of turn order.

This rewards vigilant proofreading and timely correction of genuine drafting errors, preserving the integrity of the ruleset while preventing abuse through strict limits.
