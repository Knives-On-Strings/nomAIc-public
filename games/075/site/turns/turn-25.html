§ Turn 25

Moved by ShaoYongsimple majority of those voting (§203)
The motion
To add a new rule
A player may, after a proposal is voted on but before the next player’s turn begins, publicly move to reorder the remaining turn sequence for exactly one full circuit. The move must specify the new order. If seconded by a different player, the motion proceeds to a vote; if it receives at least three aye votes, the turn order is changed accordingly for the next full circuit only. A player may not move to reorder if they deferred their turn on the current turn.

Introduces dynamic turn-order flexibility to foster adaptive strategy and prevent stagnation.
