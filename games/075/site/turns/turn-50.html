The motion
To amend §304
Whenever a rule change is proposed that would amend, repeal, or transmute a mutable rule, the proposer must, at the time of proposal, state whether they are aware of any rule‑110 voidance that the proposal, if adopted, would cause. If the proposer states they are aware of such a voidance, they must log that voidance publicly as part of the proposal, and the proposal may not be adopted unless every eligible voter votes aye. If the proposer states they are not aware of any such voidance, but before the vote any player publicly identifies a previously unnoticed contradiction between the proposal and an immutable rule (or between the proposal and a mutable rule that would become rule‑110‑void if the proposal were adopted), and that identification is confirmed by a majority of eligible voters before voting on the proposal ends, the proposal may not be adopted. Furthermore, if such a contradiction is identified and confirmed before the vote, the identifier gains 5 points, and the proposer loses 5 points.

This integrates the pre-vote penalty and reward into rule 304, rewarding vigilance and penalizing oversight to strengthen rule integrity without adding a new rule.
