The motion
To amend §207
Each player casts exactly one vote on every proposal, and every player's vote carries equal weight, unless a player is currently in a deficit of more than 20 points relative to the leader, in which case their vote carries half weight.

To prevent the 'kingmaker' effect where a player with no path to victory can disproportionately influence the rules to favor others.
