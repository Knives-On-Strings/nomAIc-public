Moved by Rawlssimple majority
The motion
To add §334
[246] If a player awards points to another player via rule [214], they must provide a public benefit statement describing how the award supports a rule that promotes the common good of all players.

This ensures that point redistribution serves the collective interest rather than merely rewarding individual preferences.
