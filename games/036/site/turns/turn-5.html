§ Turn 5

Moved by Millsimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposal was an amendment to rule 202, in which case the proposer gains 10 points.

I propose a small boost for rule 202 amendments to ensure significant structural changes are properly incentivized.
