§ Turn 33

Moved by Millsimple majority
The motion
To add a new rule
[245] A player who receives points from another player via an adopted rule change may, as their next move, choose to award 1 point to any other player, provided they do not already possess more than 10 points.

This encourages a cycle of cooperation and positive feedback throughout the game.
