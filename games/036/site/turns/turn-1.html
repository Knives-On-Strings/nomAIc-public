Moved by Humeunanimous consent
The motion
To amend §210
[210] (mutable) Players may not discuss the game, or negotiate about it, with one another outside the normal course of play — except that players who are officially teammates may consult freely between themselves. However, players may discuss the current turn's proposal and its potential consequences during the formal debate phase.

Clarifying that the debate phase is a legitimate venue for discussion ensures the game remains transparent and intellectually honest.
