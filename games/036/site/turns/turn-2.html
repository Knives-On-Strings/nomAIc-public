Moved by Millunanimous consent
The motion
To amend §203
A rule change is adopted only when every eligible voter votes aye — unanimous consent — except that once turn order has carried play through two full circuits of every player, the threshold permanently drops to a simple majority of those voting. If a proposal is adopted under this simple majority threshold, the proposer gains points equal to the number of 'aye' votes received, but does not receive points for any 'nay' votes.

This compromise rewards cooperation and encourages players to find common ground to reach the majority threshold.

The floor

I would prefer we maintain the integrity of rule [202] to ensure that the evolution of our laws remains driven by their utility rather than a calculation of popularity.
