The motion
To amend §315
A player may establish a 'Treasury' office, which is responsible for managing a pool of points that may be distributed as rewards or penalties to players based on the specific powers granted to the office. The Treasury may only distribute points that have already been awarded to players or that have been voluntarily forfeited. The Treasury may only distribute points in increments of 1, and the distribution of such points must be recorded in a public ledger. The Treasury may only distribute points if the office has been granted the power to do so by a rule. The Treasury's records shall be maintained in a public ledger that is open to inspection by all players.

This amendment clarifies the accessibility of the public ledger to ensure full transparency in Treasury operations.
