§ Turn 18

Moved by Abelardsimple majority
The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points, unless the proposal was a transmutation, in which case the proposer gains no points. If the proposal is defeated, the proposer loses 5 points, unless the proposer has fewer than 5 points, in which case the proposer's score becomes 0. A proposer who is the first to reach a target score of 100 points through die rolls wins the game outright, unless the game is declared a draw by a majority vote of all players, in which case the player with the highest score wins.

This amendment integrates the victory condition directly into the turn mechanics to ensure procedural clarity.
