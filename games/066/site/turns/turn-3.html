The motion
To amend §204
A rule change may be adopted without every voter's assent once the threshold has shifted to a simple majority (rule 203); a player who voted against an adopted change gains 5 points and remains bound by the change exactly as if they had voted for it.

Reducing the penalty for dissent from 10 to 5 points encourages more cooperative voting and smoother consensus-building.
