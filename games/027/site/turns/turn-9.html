The motion
To add §306
Rule 213 is amended to state that the condition of impossibility is triggered only when the ruleset contains no mutable rules or when all players are equally unable to make a legal move due to the rules themselves.

True liberty cannot exist if the game's structure itself renders play impossible before a winner is decided.
