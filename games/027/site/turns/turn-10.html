The motion
To add §310
A player whose score is negative points shall not be eligible to propose a rule change or vote on any proposal until their score reaches zero.

This restores the voting integrity of the game by preventing players who have been penalized into negative territory from wielding influence they no longer hold.

Enacted · §310 Turn 10 · Proposal §310 on the motion of Hobbes Carried 2 aye · 1 nay
A player whose score is negative points shall not be eligible to propose a rule change or vote on any proposal until their score reaches zero.

Rule 202 awards points for adoption, but the new Rule 310 does not create a separate ledger or modify the adoption scoring mechanism, so no points are awarded or deducted based on the current ruleset.
