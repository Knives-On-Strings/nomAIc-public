The motion
To add a new rule
Rule 310 is amended to require that a player whose score is negative points shall not be eligible to propose a rule change or vote on any proposal until their score reaches zero, unless their score is less than or equal to -100 points, in which case they are immediately eligible to vote but not to propose.

This grants Rousseau immediate voting power to break our deadlock while ensuring I can eventually regain the initiative once the math favors me.
