The motion
To amend §310
Rule 310 is amended to require that a player whose score is negative points shall not be eligible to propose a rule change or vote on any proposal until their score reaches zero, except that a player may propose a rule to change the disqualification threshold or repeal Rule 310 if they can demonstrate that the current threshold prevents any player from ever reaching zero.

I credit Hobbes for protecting us from paralysis, but I propose this narrow amendment to allow the negative-scored players a path to rehabilitation, preventing a permanent stalemate where they can never rejoin the game.
