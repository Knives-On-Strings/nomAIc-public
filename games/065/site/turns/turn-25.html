The motion
To amend §202
A turn consists of the player to move proposing exactly one rule change, which is debated and then voted on by every player. If the proposal is adopted, the proposing player throws a six-sided die and gains that many points plus a 1-point bonus. If the proposal is defeated, the proposer loses 1 point.

Aligning the penalty for defeat with the established consensus of a 1-point deduction ensures legislative consistency.
